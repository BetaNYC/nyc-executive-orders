EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 166
AMENDMENT OF EMERGENCY EVACUATION AND RE-OCCUPATION AND
TRANSPORTATION ORDER
November 1, 2012
WHEREAS, Executive Order 163 declares a state of emergency in the City of'
New York; and
WHEREAS, Executive Order 165 sets forth a prohibition on passenger vehicles
containing fewer than three persons entering Manhattan via the East River bridges, with certain
exceptions; and
WHEREAS, taxicabs and for-hire vehicles licensed by the Taxi and Limousine
Commission should be exempted from the prohibition,
NOW, THEREFORE, it is hereby ordered:
§ 1. Section 4 ofExecutive Order 165 is amended to read as follows:
§ 4. All passenger vehicles containing fewer than three persons (including the
driver) are hereby prohibited from entering Manhattan via the Brooklyn Bridge, the Manhattan
Bridge, the Ed Koch Queensboro Bridge, and the Williamsburg Bridge from 6 a.m. to midnight
on Thursday, November 1, 2012 and Friday, November 2, 2012.
The prohibition set forth
immediately above shall not include: authorized emergency vehicles; buses; paratransit vehicles:

taxicabs; for-hire vehicles licensed by the Taxi and Limousine Commission; and commercial
vehicles
§ 2. This Order shall take effect immediately.
