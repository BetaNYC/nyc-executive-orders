EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 167
REVOCATION OF EMERGENCY RESTRICTION ON EAST RIVER BRIDGES
November 2,2012
WHEREAS, Executive Order 163 declares a state of emergency in the City of
New York; and
WHEREAS, Executive Order 165 sets forth a prohibition on passenger vehicles
containing fewer than three persons entering Manhattan via the East River bridges, with certain
exceptions; and
WHEREAS, that prohibition can safely be revoked at 5 p.m. today,
NOW, THEREFORE, it is hereby ordered:
§ 1. Section 4 of Executive Order 165, as amended by Executive Order 166, is
revoked.
§ 2.
This Order shall take effect at 5 p.m. on November 2,2012.
