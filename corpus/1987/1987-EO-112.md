THE City oF NEw York
OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
NEw York, N.Y. 10007
Executive Order No. 112
April 9, 1987
Revocation of Executive Order No. 49 (Dated April 24, 1980)
and Executive Order No. 31 (Dated July 20, 1979)
By virtue of the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Prior Orders Revoked.
Executive Order No. 31, dated
July 20, 1979, and Executive Order No. 49, dated April 24, 1980, are
hereby revoked.
Section 2.
Effective Date.
This Order shall take effect
immediately.
Edward I. Koc
MAYOR
