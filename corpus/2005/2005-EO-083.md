EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 83

December 22, 2005

REVOCATION OF A PROCLAMATION OF A STATE OF EMERGENCY
REGARDING TRANSIT STRIKE

Section 1. By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, Executive
Order No. 81, dated December 20, 2005, is hereby revoked.

§ 2.
This Order shall take effect at 12:01 a.m. on December 23, 2005.

_______________________
Michael R. Bloomberg
Mayor
