MAYOR'S COMMITTEE ON THE JUDICIARY
By the poWér vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Prior Order Amended.
Paragraph (a) of Section 5 of
Executive Order Number 10 (1978) is hereby amended to read as
follows:
"§5.
Committee Membership.
(a)
The Committee
shall consist of twenty-seven members, each residing or having a principal place of business
in the City of New York, appointed by the Mayor.
The Mayor shall choose thirteen members including
the Chairman, and the Presiding Justices of the
Appellate Division for the First and Second
Judicial Departments shall each choose six
members.
Two deans of law schools within New
York City shall each choose one member, with
authority to choose rotating annually among eligible
deans.
Those members chosen by law school deans
shall serve for a term of one year; all other
members shall serve for terms of two years
commencing January 1, 1984.
Vacancies in the
Committee shall be filled in the same manner as
initial appointments.
A member of the Committee
may be removed by the Mayor for cause.
Section 2.” Prior Order Revoked.
Executive Order Number 58 (1981)
is hereby revoked.
Section 3.
Effective Date.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
Edward I. Koch
