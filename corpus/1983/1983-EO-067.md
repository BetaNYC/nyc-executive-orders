NEW YORK CITY ENERGY OFFICE
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of
New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Office Established.
The New York City
Energy Office (the "Office") is hereby established in the
Office of the Mayor.
The Office shall be headed by a
Director.
Section 2.
Responsibilities.
The Office shall, as
part of its duties, exercise all of the functions, powers
and duties of the Public Utility Service established by
Local Law No.
78 of 1982 in accordance with the provisions
of Article 14-A of the Generali Municipal Law.
Section 3.
Annual Report.
The Office shall report
annually to the Mayor on its activities and shall prepare
such other reports as the Mayor shall request.
Section 4.
Amendments.
Amendment to this Order that
transfers the functions, powers and duties of the Public
Utility Service to any other City agency shall be submitted
to the City Council and shall be subject to the action
of that body in accordance with the provisions of §ll(c)
of the City. Charter.
Section 5.
Effective Date.
This Order shall take
effect immediately.
