THE City oF New YoRK
OFFICE OF THE Mayor
New YorRK, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 37
ACQUISITION OF PROPERTY TO PROTECT THE WATER SUPPLY
July 28, 1997
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered:
Section 1.
Implementation of Mayoral Duties in Acquisition of Real Property
Outside the City of New York for Water Supply Purposes.
The Director of the Office of
Operations, and staff of such Office designated by such Director, are hereby authorized to give
preliminary approval to the acquisition of real property outside the City of New York for water
supply purposes and to arrange and conduct any public hearing to be held pursuant to section
5-358 of the Administrative Code, including required notice thereof.
§2. Approval of Maps and Payments.
The Deputy Mayor for Operations is
hereby authorized to sign the certificate of final adoption of acquisition maps pursuant to section
5-360 of the Administrative Code, and to approve amounts to be paid for the real property
identified on such maps pursuant to section 5-361 of the Administrative Code.
§3, Effective Date and Filing. This Order shall/take effect as and
shall be filed with the City Clerk upon its execution.
LA
Rudolph WW. Gidliani
Mayor
