EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 36
July 10, 1997
THE OFFICE OF SPECIAL PROJECTS AND COMMUNITY EVENTS
Section 1. There shall be in the Office of the Mayor an Office of Special Projects
and Community Events, headed by a Director who shall serve at the pleasure of the Mayor.
Section 2. The Director shall have the following duties:
a. Directing, planning, and coordinating all activities of the Office.
b. Providing necessary support
to the Commission for the United Nations, the
Consular Corps and Protocol in connection with all official receptions and ceremonies at Gracie
Mansion, City Hall and other appropriate locations.
c. Organizing, managing and directing all other public ceremonies, celebrations,
receptions, and similar functions held under City auspices by order of the Mayor.
d. Reviewing and processing requests for awards, proclamations and citations.
e. Coordinating the activities of the various City agencies and departments with
regard to the planning and implementation of special projects and community events.
f.
Maintaining liaison with business and public relations firms for the
coordination and support of City sponsored events.
Section 3. Executive Order No. 68, dated August 25, 1976 is, hereby superseded.
Section 4. This Order shall take effect immediately.
