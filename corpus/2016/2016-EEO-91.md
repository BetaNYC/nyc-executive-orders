THE CITY OF NEW YORK
OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
NEW YORK, NY 10007
EMERGENCY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 91
January 24, 2016
WHEREAS, on January 22, 2016, Emergency Executive Order No. 89 declared a state of
emergency in the City of New York due to extreme winter weather conditions; and
WHEREAS, Emergency Executive Order No. 90 , dated January 23, 2016, set forth prohibitions
on vehicular traffic within the City of New York; and
WHEREAS, the closure of the streets and highways to vehicular traffic within the City of New
York, can safely be revoked as of 7:00 a.m., January 24, 2016.
NOW, THEREFORE, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1. Sections 1 and 2 of Executive Order No. 90, restricting vehicular traffic on the streets
and highways of the City, are hereby revoked.
Section 2. The public is urged to avoid all unnecessary driving and remain off the roads effective
Sunday, January 24, 2016, 7:00 a.m. to midnight and to use public transportation whenever possible.
Members of the public who must drive due to an emergency shall use extreme caution.
Section 3. Executive Order No. 89, declaring a state of local emergency in the City and
prohibiting the blocking of roadways or impeding the ability to plow streets, remains in effect.
Section 4. This order shall take effect immediately and shall be deemed to have been in effect as
of 7:00 a.m. on January 24, 2016.
