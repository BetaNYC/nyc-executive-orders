THE City OF New YORK
OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
New York, NY 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 46
January 13, 2025
DEPUTY MAYORS AND SENIOR LEADERSHIP
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1. There shall be eight Deputy Mayors. One shall be designated the First Deputy
Mayor, one shall be designated the Deputy Mayor for Operations, one shall be designated the Deputy
Mayor for Health and Human Services, one shall be designated the Deputy Mayor for Strategic
Initiatives, one shall be designated the Deputy Mayor for Public Safety, one shall be designated the
Deputy Mayor for Communications, one shall be designated the Deputy Mayor for Administration,
who shall also be referred to as Chief of Staff, and one shall be designated the Deputy Mayor for
Intergovernmental Affairs. There shall also be a Chief Counsel to the Mayor and City Hall.

government of the City of New York and the exercise of the powers and duties of the Mayor.

promote quality and affordable housing; the economic development or growth of the City, including
those relating to the City’s overall business climate; the attraction, retention and expansion of
domestic and global business enterprises in the City; commercial, industrial and residential
development; waterfront development; energy, foreign trade and investment; film, theater and
broadcasting; and tourism.

collaboration, and ensure that policies, operations, and administration of government are consistent
