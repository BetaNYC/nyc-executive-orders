EMERGENCY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 65
EXTENSION OF EMERGENCY ORDER REGARDING WAIVER OF CERTAIN LOCAL LAWS
RELATED TO DEMOLITION
September 3, 2014
WHEREAS Emergency Executive Order 61, issued August 19, 2014, contains a
proclamation extending a state of emergency in the City of New York, and such a proclamation remains
in effect for a period not to exceed thirty days or until rescinded, whichever occurs first; and
WHEREAS, on October 26, 2012, the Governor issued a Declaration of Emergency for
all counties in the State of New York, including the City of New York and contiguous counties, and on
August 6, 2014, extended such Declaration for the counties of Bronx, Kings, New York, Queens and
Richmond and such declaration remains in effect for 180 days;
WHEREAS, Executive Order 234, issued February 5, 2013, contains an emergency order
regarding waiver of certain local laws related to demolition;
NOW, THERERORE, it is hereby ordered:
§ 1. The emergency order regarding waiver of certain local laws related to demolition set
forth in Executive Order 234 and subsequently extended most recently in Emergency Executive Order 64
dated August 29, 2014, is hereby extended for five days.
§ 2. This Order shall take effect immediately. It shall remain in effect for (5) days unless
