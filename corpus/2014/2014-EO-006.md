OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
New York, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 6
DESIGNATION OF THE NEW YORK CITY HUMAN RESOURCES ADMINISTRATION AS THE
ADMINISTERING AGENCY OF THE NEW YORK CITY IDENTITY CARD PROGRAM
August 4, 2014
WHEREAS, Local Law No. 35 for the year 2014, which amends subchapter | of chapter | of title
3 of the Administrative Code of the City of New York by adding
a new section 3-115, in relation to the
creation of
a New York city identity card program, was enacted on July 10, 2014;
WHEREAS, Local Law No. 35 requires the Mayor to designate an agency to administer the New
York city identity card program; and
WHEREAS, Local Law No. 35 provides that it shall take effect six months after enactment, but
also provides that the administering agency may take such measures as are necessary for the
implementation of the law prior to such date;
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered:
Section 1. The Human Resources Administration is designated as the administering agency of the
New York City identity card program.
§ 2. This Order shall take effect immediately.
Bill de Blasio
Mayor
