EMERGENCY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 51
EXTENSION OF EMERGENCY ORDER REGARDING WAIVER OF CERTAIN LOCAL
LAWS RELATED TO DEMOLITION
July 5, 2014
WHEREAS Emergency Executive Order 47, issued June 20, 2014, contains a
proclamation extending a state of emergency in the City of New York, and such a proclamation
remains in effect for a period not to exceed thirty days or until rescinded, whichever occurs first;
and
WHEREAS, on October 26, 2012, the Governor issued a Declaration of
Emergency for all counties in the State of New York, including the City of New York and
contiguous counties, and on June 13, 2014 extended such Declaration for the counties of Bronx,
Kings, New York, Queens and Richmond retroactive to February 8, 2014, and such declaration
remains in effect for 180 days;
WHEREAS, Executive Order 234, issued February 5, 2013, contains an
emergency order regarding waiver of certain local laws related to demolition;
NOW, THERERORE, it is hereby ordered:
§ 1. The emergency order regarding waiver of certain local laws related to
demolition set forth in Executive Order 234 is hereby extended for five days.
