EMERGENCY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 5
EXTENSION OF EMERGENCY ORDER REGARDING WAIVER OF CERTAIN
LOCAL LAWS RELATED TO DEMOLITION
January 11, 2014
WHEREAS Executive Order 460, issued December 22, 2013, contains a
proclamation extending a state of emergency in the City of New York, and such a
proclamation remains in effect for a period not to exceed thirty days or until rescinded,
whichever occurs first; and
WHEREAS, on October 26, 2012, the Governor issued a Declaration of
Emergency for all counties in the State of New York, including the City of New York
and contiguous counties, and on October 11, 2013 extended such Declaration for certain
counties including the City of New York, and such declaration remains in effect for 120
days;
WHEREAS, Executive Order 234, issued February 5, 2013, contains an
emergency order regarding waiver of certain local laws related to demolition;
NOW, THERERORE, it is hereby ordered:
§ 1. The emergency order regarding waiver of certain local laws related to
demolition set forth in Executive Order 234 is hereby extended for five days.

§ 2. This Order shall take effect immediately. It shal] remain in effect for
(5) days unless it is terminated or modified at an earlier date.
