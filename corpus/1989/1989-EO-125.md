EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 125
December 4, 1989
EXECUTIVE ORDER IMPOSING
A CHARGE FOR
PAYMENTS TO THE CITY BY CHECKS RETURNED
FOR INSUFFICIENT FUNDS
By virtue of the power vested in me as Mayor of the
City of New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Charge of Payment on Account of
Insufficient Funds.
Pursuant to section 85 of the General
Municipal Law, a charge of fifteen dollars per check may be
added to any account owing to the city of New York or any
of its agencies where a tendered payment of such account was
by a check or other written order that was returned for
insufficient funds.
Section 2.
Effective Date.
This Order shall take
effect 30 days after publication in the City Record.
