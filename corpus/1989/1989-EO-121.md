ESTABLISHMENT OF A PLANNING UNIT FOR
PURPOSES OF PREPARATION AND IMPLEMENTATION
OF A LOCAL SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT PLAN
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Planning Unit Authoriz-d.
In accordance
with 6 New York Codes, Rules and Regulati'
s Subpart 360-15,
the City of New York is hereby authorized to be a planning
unit for purposes of preparing and implementing a local
solid waste management plan.
Section 2.
Signature Authorized.
The Deputy Mayor for
Policy and Physical Development, whose office is established
pursuant to Executive Order No. 73, dated April 30,
1984 is
hereby authorized to sign an application to secure grant
funds, submitted pursuant to 6 New York Codes, Rules and
Regulations Subpart 360-15, and to sign any other documents
as may be necessary to secure such funds.
Section 3.
Effective Date.
This order shall take
effect immediately.
