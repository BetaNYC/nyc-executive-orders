THE City oF NEw Yor
OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
New York, N.Y. 10007
Executive Order No.
119
February 27, 1989
COMMUNITY RELATIONS STRATEGIC PLANNING GROUP
WHEREAS, New York City cherishes its racial, ethnic and
cultural diversity; and
WHEREAS,
New York City derives its greatest benefits
from this diversity when its racial, ethnic, and cultural
groups strive for harmony
and
understanding
among
one
another; and
WHEREAS,
municipal government plays an important and
positive role in fostering good relations between New York
City's racial, ethnic, and cultural groups;
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, I hereby order that a Community Relations Strategic
Planning Group be established to help further promote good
relations among New York's diverse citizenry.
Section 1.
Planning Group Established. In order to
promote
good relations between
New York City's diverse
racial, ethnic, and cultural groups, a Community Relations
Strategic Planning Group is hereby established.
Section 2.
Responsibility.
The Community Relations
Strategic Planning
Group will be
responsible for
the
coordination of
the various services provided by City
agencies that are designed to improve intergroup relations.

Section
3.
Membership.
The
Community
Relations
Strategic Planning Group will be chaired by the director of
the
Mayor's
Community
Assistance
Unit.
The
Deputy
Commissioner for Community Relations of the New York City
Commission on Human Rights and the Deputy Commissioner of
the Community Affairs Division of the New York City Police
Department will serve as vice-chairs.
The Commissioner of
the Department of Parks
& Recreation and the Directors of
the Community Development Agency,
the Mayor's Office of
Youth
Services,
and
the
Youth
Bureau will designate
representatives from their agencies to serve
as
members
until replaced by designated alternates.
The Board of Education, the Housing Authority and the
Transit Authority may
recommend
one member each to be
designated by the Mayor.
Section 4.
Convening of the Group.
The Community
Assistance Unit of the Mayor's Office will convene
the
Community Relations Strategic Planning Group semiannually,
in May and December.
Section
5.
Authorization
to
Promulgate
Rules,
Regulations and Procedures.
The heads of all the agencies
represented in the Community Relations Strategic Planning
Group shall promulgate,
as necessary, appropriate rules,
regulations
and
procedures
required to carry out the
objectives and policies of this Executive Order.
Section 6.
Effective Date.
This Order shall take
effect immediately.
Loo
Edward I. Koch
MAYOR
