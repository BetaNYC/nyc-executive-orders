EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 34
SEPTEMBER 7, 1979
BY VIRTUE OF THE POWER VESTED IN ME AS MAYOR OF THE CITY OF
New York, it is hereby ordered :
Section 1.
Stanley Brezenoff, the Human Resources Administrator, shall succeed to
the powers and duties of the Health Services Administrator as specified in Chapter 1016
of the Laws of 1969 (Section 7381 ef seq. of the Unconsolidated laws of New York).
§2.
Paragraph (b) of Section 8 of Executive Order No. 27, dated January 26, 1979,- is
hereby revoked.
