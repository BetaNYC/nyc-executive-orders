OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 31
JULY 20, 1979
CETA TRANSITION
Whereas, The City of New York, as a prime sponsor under the Comprehensive Employment
and Training Act (CETA), is to undertake efforts to transition into unsubsidized employment
its participants who exhaust their statutory period of public service employment, and
Whereas, It is necessary and desirable for the City to implement these transition efforts in
a manner consistent with federal requirements and its commitment to the City’s public service
employment workforce.
ow, therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York, it is hereby
ordered as follows:
Section 1.
Definitions. Whenever used in this Executive Order the following terms shall
have the following meanings ;
(a) CETA Workforce. “CETA Workforce” means any persons employed in positions which are supported in whole or in part by funds provided by the United States Department of Labor under CETA Titles II-D or VI, excluding positions concerned with
administration of the CETA Program.
(b) Contract, The term “contract” shall mean any written agreement or instrument
whereby the City, or any agency thereof, is committed to expend or does expend its funds in
return for work, labor, or services,
(c) Contracting Agency. The term “Contracting Agency” means any board, bureau,
department, commission or other agency of the government of
The City of New York which
is authorized to and does, on behalf of the City, provide for, enter into, award, or administer
contracts.
§2.
Responsibility of Agency Heads. All agency heads shall conduct and maintain a survey
of their CETA Workforce, and shall give consideration to qualified persons from that workforce
when making placements into unsubsidized tax levy positions which may from time to time become available and for which there may be hiring without regard to outstanding eligible lists or
provisions of an existing collective bargaining agreement.
§3.
Contract Provsions, Every Contracting Agency shail include in every City contract
hereafter entered into, the following provision:
“The Contractor shall, when hiring into unsubsidized jobs in its workforce, give consideration to those persons presently employed in CETA public service positions in The
City of New York, whose qualifications and skills are commensurate with those required
for the positions to be filled.”
$4.
Reports of Agency Heads. The Department of Employment and the Department of
Personnel will establish a procedure by which agency heads will report on a monthly basis to
the Deputy Mayor for Administration the steps taken in furtherance of this Executive Order.
ge Public Agencies Within The City of New York. Any board, authority, commission,
or other public agency, not part of the City government, whose field of operations and jurisdiction lie solely within The City of New York, is requested to comply with this Executive Order.
Effective Date. This Order shail take effect immediately.
