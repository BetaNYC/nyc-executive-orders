OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
14 299 ys
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 33
JULY 31, 1979
REVOCATION OF EXECUTIVE ORDERS
BY THE POWER VESTED IN ME AS MAYOR OF THE CITY OF NEW YORK,
it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Prior Orders Revoked.
Executive Order No. 48 (1975) and Executive Order
No. 66 (1976) are hereby revoked.
§ 2.
Effective Date.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
EDWARD I. KOCH, Mayor.
