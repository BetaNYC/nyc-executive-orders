OFFICE OF THE Mayor
New Yorn, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 3
January 2, 1990
MAYOR'S COMMITTEE ON THE JUDICIARY
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Prior Order Amended.
Executive Order Number
10 (1978), as amended, is hereby further amended by adding a new
section 9 to read as follows:
§9.
Corporation Counsel.
The Corporation Counsel shall
maintain liaison with and review the activities of the Committee, and
shall advise the Mayor and the Committee regarding measures which
may
enhance
the
ability of
the
Committee
to
consider
the
best
qualified candidates for judicial nomination.
Section 2.
Effective Date.
This Order shall take effect
immediately .
David
N. Dinkins
MAYOR
