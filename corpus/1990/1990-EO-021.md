EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 21
October 5_, 1990
OFFICE OF LABOR RELATIONS EXECUTIVE ORDER AMENDED
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered as follows:
Section 1. Order Amended.
Paragraph (f) of section 7 of
Executive Order No. 13, dated July 24, 1990, is amended to read as
follows:
(f)
to consult with the Office of Labor Relations before
any City department or agency takes disciplinary action against
any employee, or group of employees, involved in a labor dispute.
§2.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
*
David N. Dinkins
MAYOR
