THe City of New YoRK
OFFICE OF THE Mayor
New Yorn, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 19
September
19, 1990
MAYOR'S ADVISORY COMMITTEE ON APPOINTMENTS
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Executive Order Number 5 (1990) is amended by
renumbering section 9 thereof as section 10 and adding a new section
9 to read as follows:
§9.
Counsel to the Mayor.
The Counsel to the Mayor shall
maintain liaison with and review the activities of the Committee, and
shall advise the Mayor and the Committee regarding measures which
may
enhance
the
ability
of
the
Committee
to
consider
the
best
qualified candidates for appointment to commissions, boards and other
public bodies.
Section 2.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
David N. Dinkins
MAYOR
