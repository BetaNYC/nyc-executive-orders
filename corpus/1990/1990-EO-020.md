THE City of NEw York
OFFICE OF THE Mayor
NEw Yorn, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 20
September 28, 1990
PAYMENT OF CLAIMS OF CERTAIN EMPLOYEES
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:
Section
1.
The
Director
of
the
Mayor's
Office
of
Administrative Services is
authorized
to
conduct
all inquiries
and
hearings, make all determinations,
and authorize all payments which
may be conducted,
made or authorized by the
Mayor pursuant to
section 12-104 of the Administrative Code in relation to any claims as
described therein filed against the City, an agency of the City or the
Board of Education, by duly appointed employees or their estates.
§2.
This Order shall take effect immediately,
David N. Dinkins
MAYOR
