DESIGNATION OF THE RESPONSIBLE AGENCY FOR
FRANCHISES TO BE AWARDED PURSUANT TO THE CITY CHARTER
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1:
Pursuant to §§363 and 378 of the New York City
Charter:
(a)
The Department of the Transportation is
designated the responsible agency for the
following types of franchises -~
(1)
Bus lines,
(2)
Bus stop shelters,
(3)
Electric power distribution systems,
(4)
Tramways,
(5)
Below grade passenger railroad tracks and
tunnels.
(b)
The Department of Ports and Trade is
designated the responsible agency for the
following types of franchises --
(1)
Railroad freight and shipping lines,
(2)
Refrigeration distribution systems.
