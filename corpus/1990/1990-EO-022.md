THE City oF New York
OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
NEw Yorn, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO.
22
November 28 , 1990
MAYOR'S OFFICE OF VETERANS! AFFAIRS (MOVA)
WHEREAS, the Mayor's Office of Veterans' Affairs (MOVA)
was created by Local Law No.
53 for the year 1987 to better assess
the
needs
of
New
York
City's
veteran
population,
and _
better
coordinate service delivery to veterans and their families; and
WHEREAS,
certain
Mayoral
agencies
are
responsible
for
providing a variety of social services to address the needs of our
citizens,
such
as
public
assistance,
employment
and _
training,
housing, health, medical and rehabilitation services; and
WHEREAS, a significant percentage of the clients of these
Mayoral agencies are veterans of the Armed Forces of the United
States and their families; and
WHEREAS,
veterans
of
the
Armed
Forces
comprise
a
significant percentage of the employees of the City of New York; and
WHEREAS, these veterans may have special needs and there
are services available to address these needs; and
WHEREAS, the Director of MOVA is mandated to address
these issues;

NOW, THEREFORE, in order to assist the Director of MOVA
to fulfill its mandate and effectively develop initiatives on behalf of
New York City's veterans, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the
City of New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section
1.
Agency
Coordinators for Veterans'
Services.
The Commissioner or Director of the following agencies shall appoint a
staff member
of appropriate
managerial authority
whose
duty
(in
addition to his or her other responsibilities) it shall be to coordinate
veterans' services within the relevant agency and serve as
liaison
with other public and private agencies providing veterans’ services.
Such
a_
staff
member
shall
be
appointed
by
the
Director
or
Commissioner of the:
(1)
Human Resources Administration
(2)
Department for the Aging
(3)
Department of Consumer Affairs
(4)
Department of Correction
(5)
Office of Economic and Financial Opportunity
(6)
Department of Employment
(7)
Department of Finance
(8)
Department of Health
(8)
Office on Homelessness and SRO Housing
(10) Department of Housing Preservation and Development
(11) Office of Mental Health, Mental Retardation, and
Alcoholism Services
(12) Department of Personnel
(13) Office for People with Disabilities
