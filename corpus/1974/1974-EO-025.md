OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 25
DECEMBER 12, 1974
AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO li
DATED MARCH 21, 1974
WHEREAS, it is necessary and desirable to amend Executive Order No. 11, outlining
the duties and functions of the Criminal Justice Coordinating Council.
NOW, THEREF ORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York,
it is hereby ordered as follows:
Section I.
Section § of the Executive Order No, 11, dated March 21, 1974, is hereby
amended to read as follows:
"§5.
The Mayor shall appoint an Executive Committee of not more than twentythree (23) members which shall act-on behalf of the Council.”
§2.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
