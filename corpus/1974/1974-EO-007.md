EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 7
JANUARY 22, 1974
AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 2
(DATED JANUARY I, 1974).
Whereas, It is necessary and desirable to amend Executive Order No. 2, outlining
the duties and responsibilities of the three Deputy Mayors:
Now, therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York, it is
hereby ordered as follows:
Section 1.
Section § of Executive Order No, 2, dated January 1, 1974, is hereby
amended to read as follows:
“S$,
Executive Order No. 71, dated April 2, 1968, Executive Order No. 20, dated
uly 35, 1970, Executive Order No. 31, dated January 18, 1971, and Executive Order
lo. 74, dated January 23, 1973, are amended so as to delete therefrom references to
Deputy Mayor—City Administrator and City Administrator and insert therein the
designation Deputy
Mayor wherever such deletions are made pursuant to this order.”
§2.
This order shali take effect immediately.
