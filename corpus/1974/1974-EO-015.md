OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 15
June 20, 1974
COOPERATION BY THE CITY WITH THE NEW YORK
CITY STABILIZATION RESERVE CORPORATION
Whereas, The Legislature has established the New York City Stabilization Reserve
Corporation to enable the City to provide essential services during the City fiscal years
1973-1974 and 1974-1975; and
hereas, Section 2548 of the New York City Stabilization Reserve Corporation Act
(L. 1974, Ch. 571) provides that such Corporation may, with the consent of the City, use
agents, employees and facilities of the City ; and
hereas, It is in the interest of the City
to enable such Corporation to organize and
operate as expeditiously and efficiently as possible ;
Now, therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York, it is
hereby ordered as follows:
Section 1.
The New York City Stabilization Reserve Corporation is authorized
to use the employees and facilities of the City, including those of the Office of the
Mayor, the Bureau of the Budget and the Corporation Counsel to carry on its corparate purposes,
2. All agency heads are directed to cooperate with the New York Stabilization
Reserve Corporation and to provide such employees and facilities of the City to such
Corporation as are necessary for the Corporation ‘a,carry on its corporate purposes.
$3. This Executive Order shall take effect mon dtely.
