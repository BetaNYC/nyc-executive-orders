Section 1,
There is hereby created a Division of Management Analysis in the Bureau
of the Budget, Office of the Mayor.
;
§2. The Division of Management Analysis shall have the following functions and
duties:
(a) to undertake management surveys and analytical studies of agency operations
and make recommendations to raise the level of the efficiency of government services
by improved employee utilization, methods and procedures and performance reporting
systems.
(b)
to perform audits of agency operations which will support the Bureau of the
Budget's continuing review of agency fiscal operations and controls.
(c) to evaluate the feasibility and cost effectiveness agency computer based information systems, including
contract management, design and implementation.
§3.
