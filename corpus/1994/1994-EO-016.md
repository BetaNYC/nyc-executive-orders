EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 16
December 27, 1994
AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 2
DATED JANUARY 1, 1994, RELATING TO
DEPUTY MAYORS
By the power vested in me as the Mayor of the City of New York, it hereby
is ordered:
Section 1].
Executive Order No. 2, dated January 1, 1994, as amended by
Executive Order No. 9, dated June 17, 1994, is amended to reletter subdivision k as
subdivision m and to add new subdivisions k and | to section 6 to read as follows:
k.
Perform any function, power or duty of the Mayor in relation to the
execution of deeds, leases, easements, mortgages and any other grants of estates or interests
in real property and licenses, permits or other agreements affecting the use of real property
by the Department of Housing Preservation and Development, the Economic Development
Corporation, the New York City Industrial Development Agency, or the Brooklyn Navy
Yard Development Corporation and in relation to any contracts with or involving the
Economic Development Corporation, the New York City Industrial Development Agency,
or the Brooklyn Navy Yard Development Corporation.
1.
Execute all authorizations, approvals and certifications to be made or
given by the Mayor pursuant to sections 384, 1602 and 1802 of the Charter with regard to
acquisitions and dispositions of real property involving the Department of Housing
Preservation and Development, the Economic Development Corporation, the New York
City Industrial Development Agency or the Brooklyn Navy Yard Development Corporation.
