TRANSFER OF FUNCTIONS OF NEW YORK CITY HOUSING AUTHORITY
POLICE DEPARTMENT TO NEW YORK CITY POLICE DEPARTMENT,
CONSISTENT WITH THE TERMS AND CONDITIONS
OF A MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING
WHEREAS, merger of the three separate police forces within the City of New
York would result in more effective delivery of police services to all the citizens of the City
of New York, and in particular to residents of public housing;
WHEREAS, the New York City Housing Authority (NYCHA) and the City
of New York recognize that a merger of the New York City Housing Authority Police
Department (HAPD) and the New York City Police Department (NYPD), subject to
appropriate terms and conditions, would serve the best interests of the residents of public
housing and the City of New York;
WHEREAS, the New York City Housing Authority and the City of New York
intend to enter into
a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) containing the terms and
conditions of a merger of the HAPD and the NYPD; and
WHEREAS, such MOU will provide for such merger to take place by October
1, 1994, or as soon as possible thereafter;
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it hereby is ordered:
Section 1. Effective October 1, 1994, or as soon as possible thereafter as a
merger of the New York City Police Department and the New York City Housing Authority
Police Department may be effectuated pursuant to the terms and conditions of a duly
executed Memorandum of Understanding between the New York City Housing Authority

and the City of New York, the New York City Police Department shall assume the functions
of the Housing Authority Police Department and uniformed housing police force thereof
consistent with such terms and conditions.
Section 2. The City Personnel Director and Police Commissioner shall take
all steps necessary, consistent with applicable law, to implement Section 1 of this Order,
including ensuring the transfer from the New York City Housing Authority to the New York
City Police Department of uniformed and civilian employees substantially engaged in the
performance of the functions of the New York City Housing Authority Police Department.
