THe City oF NEw YORK
OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
New YorRK,
N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 15
October2S, 1994
TRANSFER OF FUNCTIONS OF CERTAIN CLINICAL PROGRAMS OF THE
NEW YORK CITY DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH TO THE
NEW YORK CITY HEALTH AND HOSPITALS CORPORATION, CONSISTENT
WITH THE TERMS AND CONDITIONS OF AGREEMENTS
WHEREAS,
the
transfer
of
five
(5)
New
York
City
Department of Health clinical programs encompassing Child Health,
Dental Health, Correctional Health, Communicare and Homeless Health
(hereinafter the
'"Programs")
to the
New York City Health and
Hospitals Corporation would result in more effective delivery of
primary medical care services to the citizens of the City of New
York; and
WHEREAS,
the City of New York and the New York City
Health and Hospitals Corporation recognize that the transfer of the
Programs, subject to appropriate terms and conditions, would serve
the best interests of the residents of the City of New York; and
WHEREAS,
the City of New York and the
New York City
Health and Hospitals Corporation intend to enter into agreements
containing the terms and conditions for the provision of services
by the Programs; and
WHEREAS, such agreements will provide for the transfer of
the programs to take place by November 27,
1994, or as soon as
possible thereafter;
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the
City of New York, it hereby is ordered:
Section 1.
Effective November 27,
1994, or as soon as
possible
thereafter
as
a
transfer
of
said
Programs
may _
be
effectuated pursuant to the terms and conditions of duly executed
agreements between the City of New York and the New York City
Health
and
Hospitals
Corporation,
the
New
York City
Health

Department shall transfer to the New York City Health and Hospitals
Corporation the functions of the "Programs" and the employees
thereof consistent with such terms and conditions.
Section 2.
The City Personnel Director and
Health
Commissioner shall
take all steps necessary,
consistent with
applicable law, to implement Section
1
of this Order, including
ensuring the transfer from the New York City Department of Health
to the New York City Health and Hospitals Corporation of employees
substantially engaged in the performance of the functions of the
Programs.
Section 3.
This Order shall
Kate effect immediately.
