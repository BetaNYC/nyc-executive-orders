THe City oF NEw YORK
OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
New YorRK, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 9
June 17, 1994
AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 16,
DATED AUGUST 31, 1990, RELATING TO THE
OFFICE OF CONTRACTS, AND OF
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 2, DATED
JANUARY 1, 1994, RELATING TO
DEPUTY MAYORS
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it hereby is
ordered:
Section 1. Paragraph (v) of subdivision b of section 2 of Executive Order No.
16, dated August 31, 1990, is amended to read as follows:
(v) execute all authorizations, approvals and certifications to be made or
given by the Mayor pursuant to sections 384, 704, 1602 and 1802 of the Charter with regard
to acquisitions and dispositions of real property.
§ 2. Paragraph (vi) of subdivision b of section 2 of Executive Order No. 16,
dated August 31, 1990, is REPEALED.
§ 3. Executive Order No. 2, dated January 1, 1994, is amended to reletter
subdivision j of section 6 as subdivision k, and to add a new subdivision j to such section to
read as follows:

j.
Perform any function, power or duty of the Mayor in relation to the
execution of deeds and satisfactions in connection with the agencies specified in subdivision
c of this section.
§ 4. This Order shall take effect immediately.
(—
Rudolph W. Géuliani
