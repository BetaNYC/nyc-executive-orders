THE City OF NEW YoRK
OFFICE OF THE Mayor
NEw YoRK, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 79
October 5, 1984
Health Assistant to the Mayor
By virtue of the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York,
it is ordered:
Section 1.
Office Established.
There is established in the Office of the
Mayor, the Office of Health Assistant to the Mayor ("Office").
Section 2.
Funetions.
The Office shall be coordinated by the Health
Assistant to the Mayor who shall advise the Mayor, and the Deputy Mayor for
Operations on matters relating to health, and assist the Deputy Mayor for
Operations in maintaining liaison with and reviewing the activities for the
Health and Hospitals Corporation of the City of New York.
The Health
Assistant shall also be Deputy Health Services Administrator as specified in
Chapter 1016 of the Laws of 1969 (Section 7381 et, seq. of the Unconsolidated
Laws of New York) whenever the Health Services Administrator is for any
reason unable to attend to the duties of his office.
Section 3.
Effective Date.
This order shall be effective immediately.
Olea
Edward I. Koch
