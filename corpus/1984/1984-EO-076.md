THE City OF New YORK
OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
New York, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 176
April 30, 1984
Amendment of Executive Order No. 68,
Dated August 25, 1976
Mayor's Commission For Protocol
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered;
Section 1.
Prior Order Amended.
Section 1 and sub-section la of
Executive Order No. 68, dated August 25, 1976 are hereby amended to delete
any reference to "the Mayor's Commission for Distinguished Guests" and
substitute therefore "the Mayor's Commission for Protocol", and shall read as
follows:
"Section 1.
There is hereby established in the Office of the
Mayor, the Mayor's Commission for Protocol.
The Mayor shall appoint a Chairman and members of the
Commission who shall serve at the pleasure of the Mayor.
The duties of the Chairman shall inelude but not be limited
to the following:
a.
As Chairman of the Mayor's Commission for Protocol
he shall act as special advisor to the Mayor on ail
matters involving the ceremonial forms and courtesies
in the official City greeting of heads of State and
distinguished visitors,"
Section 2.
Effective Date.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
Edward
I. Koch
MAYOR
