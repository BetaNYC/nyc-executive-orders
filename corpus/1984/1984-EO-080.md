Traffie and Construction Coordination
Policy Statement
Because the City is now facing unprecedented traffic volumes and a
record number of private and public construction programs, it is important that
all agencies and departments cooperate to minimize traffic disruptions.
Where
in the past, lane closings may have had little effect on the City's overall
traffic flow, they now represent serious threats to the traffic system.
1.
Prior Approval.
All agency and department heads must receive the
‘prior approval of the Department of Transportation's Traffic Intelligence
Division before proceeding with planned activities which will disrupt traffie.
2.
Emergency Circumstances.
In those instances where emergency or
unplanned activities will disrupt traffic, the ageney or department head is
required to inform the Traffic Intelligence Division so that appropriate traffic
mitigation measures can be taken.
3.
Effective Date.
This Directive shall take effect immediately.
