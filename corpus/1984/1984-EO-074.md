THE CITY OF NEw YoRK
OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
New Yorn, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO, 74
April 30, 1984
Director of Management and Budget
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
ordered:
va
Section 1.
Authority.
The Director of Management and Budget shall have
the same authority to act for, on behalf of and at the direction of the Mayor,
as provided by §§ 2(n), 3(m), and 4(g) of Executive Order No. 73 (1984), which
state:
"Act for, on behalf of and at the direction of the Mayor in the
exercise of all functions, powers and duties which the Mayor may
have pursuant to the New York State Local Finance Law, as the
same may from time to time be amended, the New York State
Financial Emergency Act for the City of New York, as the same
may from time to time be amended, the New York City Loan
Guatantee Act of 1978, P.L. 95-339, as the same may from time to
time be amended, an Agreement to Guarantee by and among the
United States of America, the State of New York, the City of New
York, the New York State Financial Control Board and the Municipal
Assistance Corporation for the City of New York, a Guaranteed
Bond Purchase Agreement by and among the City of New York,
certain City pension funds, certain State pension funds and the
United States of American, each dated as of November 15, 1978, and
any seasonal financing agreement of the City of New York,
including, but not limited to, full power to execute for, on behalf
of, and at the direction of the Mayor, any document pursuant to any
Such agreement and any agreement, amendment to such agreement or
other document pursuant to such laws,
The exercise of functions,
powers and duties for, on behalf of, and at the direction of the
Mayor pursuant to this delegation shall not require the consent in
writing of the Mayor or any other official of the City."
Section 2.
Prior Order Revoked,
Prior Executive Order No. 62, dated
October 26, 1981, is hereby revoked.
®
Section 3.
Effective Date.
This order shall take effect immediately.
Edward [. Koch
