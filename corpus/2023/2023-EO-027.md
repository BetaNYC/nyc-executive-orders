EXECIJTIVE ORDER NO,27
February 16,2023
CHIEF PUBLIC REALM OFFICER
WHEREAS public space is a vital piece of the City's infrastructure that affects quality of
life for people throughout the City of New York; and
WHEREAS roadways, curbs, sidewalks, plazas, parks, and green space are among the
public spaces that constitute the City's public realm; and
WHEREAS many City agencies have jurisdiction or control over portions of the public
realm that require inter-agency coordination to execute policies and projects; and
WHEREAS external parties such as business improvement districts, nonprofits, and
private entities collaborate with the City to manage and improve public spaces for New Yorkers;
and
WHEREAS a central position within City government for visioning, planning and
maintaining the multiple dimensions of public realm will streamline public realm investments
and increase equity, safety, and access to public space for all New Yorkers;
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered that:
Section 1. There shall be a Chief Public Realm Officer, appointed by the Mayor, who
shall report to the Deputy Mayor for Operations.
$ 2. Duties. The Chief Public Realm Officer shall: (a) serve as a central point of contact
for external partners that work with the City to improve public spaces; (b) lead inter-agency
coordination and collaboration to advance policies and projects that improve public spaces
throughout New York City; and (c) drive reform and innovation in the City's public realm
strategy to improve the user experience and pilot new ways to create beautiful, vibrant public
spaces.
$ 3. Cooperation. All mayoral agencies shall cooperate with the Chief Public Realm
Offrcer in the performance of the duties described in Section 2. Upon request of the Chief Public
Realm Officer, mayoral agencies shall designate a liaison to work with the Chief Public Realm

Officer. The Chief Public Realm Officer shall convene and coordinate agency liaisons as
necessary to accomplish their duties.
$ 4. This Order shall take effect immediately.
Eric Adams
Mayor
