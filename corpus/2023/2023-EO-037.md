New York, N.Y, 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 37
OFFICE OF NIGHTLIFE
November 17, 2023
WHEREAS, the City of New York is interested in promoting the local economy and
fostering programs and initiatives that support a thriving small business community in all sectors,
including the City’s nightlife industry; and
WHEREAS, Section 20-d of the City Charter was added by Local Law No. 178 of 2017 to
create the Office of Nightlife (“ONL”); and
WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 37 of 2018 established ONL within the Mayor's Office
of Media and Entertainment (“MOME”); and
WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 21 of 2022 continued ONL within MOME; and
WHEREAS, Scction 20-d of the City Charter has been amended by local law to permit
ONL to be established within the New York City Department of Small Business Services (“SBS”);
NOW THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:
Section 1. Continuation
of the Office of Nightlife. ONL is hereby continued and reestablished within SBS. The Director of ONL shall be appointed by the Mayor and shall report to
the Commissioner of Small Business Services.
§ 2. Duties. In furtherance of the powers and duties set forth in City Charter section 20-d,
ONL will serve as a central point of contact among City agencies, the nightlife industry, and New
York City residents, promoting a safe and vibrant nightlife scene beneficial to businesses and
residents across the five boroughs.
§ 3. Section 2 of Executive Order No. 21, dated July 21, 2022, is amended to read as
follows:
§ 2. MOME shall consist of: the Film Office (also known as Mayor's Office
of Film, Theatre and Broadcasting or “MOFTB”), NYC Media, the Press
Credentials Office, and an office to coordinate creative sector programs and
activities.

§ 4. Section 8 of Executive Order No. 21, dated July 21, 2022, is repealed.
§ 5. Personnel, Affected agencies shall take all necessary and timely steps to ensure that
personnel primarily performing duties for ONL are employed by SBS in accordance with this
Order.
§ 6. Effective Date. This Order shall take effect on November 26, 2023, or as soon as
practicable thereafter as personnel primarily performing duties for ONL are employed by SBS in
accordance with this Order.
Eric Adams
Mayor
