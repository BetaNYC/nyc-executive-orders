EMERGENCY EXECIJTIVE ORDER NO . 428
June 9, 2023
WHEREAS, the public safety was imperiled by a flash flood emergency caused by the
remnants of Hurricane Ida that flooded roads, impacted mass transit, stranded motorists, and
caused widespread damage to residential and commercial buildings throughout the City, causing
unsafe conditions in those buildings and imperiling health and safety; and
WHEREAS, the state of emergency to address the remnants of Hurricane Ida, first declared
in Emergency Executive OrderNo. 230, dated September I,202I,and last extended by Emergency
Executive OrderNo. 412, dated May 20,2023, remains in effect;
NOW THEREFORE, pursuant to the powers vested in me by the laws of the State of New
York and the City of New York, including but not limited to the New York Executive Law, the
New York City Charter and the Administrative Code of the City of New York, and the Gommon
law authority to protect the public in the event of an emergency:
Section 1. I hereby direct that section 1 of Emergency Executive Order No. 424, dated June
4,2023, is extended for five (5) days.
$ 2. This Emergency Executive Order shall take effect immediately and shall remain in
effect for hve (5) days unless it is terminated or modified at an earlier date.
Eric Adams
Mayor
