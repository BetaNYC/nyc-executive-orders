EXECUTIVE ORDER 130
July 6, 2009
AUTHORIZATION TO APPLY FOR FUNDING THROUGH THE AMERICAN
RECOVERY AND REINVESTMENT ACT OF 2009 –
STRENGTHENING COMMUNITIES FUND

WHEREAS, a portion of the funding made available pursuant to the American
Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 (“ARRA”) was allocated to the Strengthening
Communities Fund (“SCF”), which is administered by the United States Department of
Health and Human Services through its Administration for Children and Families; and
WHEREAS, the assets of the SCF are to be disbursed to states, cities, counties or
Native American Tribal government offices for the purpose of facilitating government
partnerships with local non-profit organizations; and
WHEREAS, it is the role of the Office of the Mayor to ensure the effectiveness
and integrity of the operations of the New York City (“the City”) government and to
maintain policies and procedures necessary thereto; and
WHEREAS, pursuant to this mandate, it is in the best interest of the City to build
its government’s capacity to partner with non-profit organizations to coordinate an
effective response to the current economic recession; and
WHEREAS, the evaluation of the effectiveness of programs and services
provided by non-profit organizations throughout the City is critical to the allocation of
resources needed to support these programs and services; and
WHEREAS, a web-based tool to standardize such evaluation would not only
facilitate joint-efforts with the City but also conserve limited resources and enhance
capacity while greatly alleviating the burdens borne by non-profit organizations; and
WHEREAS, a grant from the SCF will provide the resources needed to develop a
web-based tool to standardize the evaluation of health and human services-related
programs, ensuring their success and long-term impact;

NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered that:
Section 1: The Office of the Mayor is hereby authorized to apply for a grant from
the SCF, made available by the United States Department for Health and Human Services
through its Administration for Children and Families.
§ 2.
The Office of the Mayor, through the Office of the Deputy Mayor for
Health and Human Services, shall be responsible for the administration of the SCF
Capacity Building program grant award, and shall conduct programmatic and financial
oversight necessary thereto, pursuant to the work plan detailed in the SCF grant
application submitted by the Office of the Mayor. The Deputy Mayor for Health and
Human Services shall further ensure that the Office of the Mayor participates in
evaluation studies and grantee conferences as required.
§ 3.
The Office of the Mayor shall have the appropriate support, access and
authority necessary to administer the SCF Capacity Building program grant award
pursuant to the work plan detailed in its SCF grant application.
§ 4.
Effective Date. This order shall take effect immediately.

Michael R. Bloomberg
Mayor
