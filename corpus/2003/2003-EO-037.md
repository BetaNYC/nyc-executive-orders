EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 37

JULY 9, 2003

MAYOR’S VOLUNTEER CENTER

By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered:
Section 1. The Mayor's Voluntary Action Center is hereby re-named the Mayor's
Volunteer Center.
§ 2. The Mayor's Volunteer Center shall bring together individuals, corporations,
government agencies and not-for-profit organizations in order to connect people with
meaningful volunteer programs that significantly improve the quality of life in New York
City. The Mayor's Volunteer Center shall encourage every New Yorker to serve as a
volunteer.
§ 3. The Mayor's Volunteer Center shall have an Executive Director and such
staff as the Mayor deems appropriate.
§ 4. This Order shall take effect immediately.

______________________
Michael R. Bloomberg
Mayor
