EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 38

JULY 31, 2003

TRANSFER OF CERTAIN FUNCTIONS AND EMPLOYEES FROM THE
ADMINISTRATION FOR CHILDREN'S SERVICES TO THE DEPARTMENT OF
SOCIAL SERVICES

WHEREAS, Local Law No. 52 for the year 2003 amends section 617 of the
Charter of the City of New York to provide that the Mayor may direct that powers and
duties related to child support enforcement and determinations of eligibility for
subsidized child care previously performed by the Administration for Children's Services
shall be performed by the Department of Social Services; and
WHEREAS, such local law takes effect on July 1, 2003 or as soon thereafter as a
transfer of agency functions may be effectuated;
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1. Effective August 24, 2003, or as soon as practicable thereafter as a
transfer may be effectuated, the Department of Social Services shall assume the functions
related to child support enforcement previously performed by the Administration for
Children's Services.
§ 2. The Commissioner of Citywide Administrative Services, the Commissioner
of Social Services and the Commissioner of the Administration for Children's Services
shall take all steps necessary, consistent with applicable law, to implement Section 1 of
this Order, including ensuring the transfer of necessary employees substantially engaged
in the performance of the functions to be assumed pursuant to this Order.
§ 3. This Order shall take effect immediately.
