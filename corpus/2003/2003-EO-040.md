EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 40

SEPTEMBER 8, 2003

DESIGNATION OF AGENCIES TO ENFORCE VEHICLE AND TRAFFIC LAW
SECTION 375 (1)

WHEREAS, chapter 231 of the laws of 2003, which amends subdivision 1 of
section 375 of the Vehicle and Traffic Law, became a law on July 29, 2003 and took
effect on September 1, 2003; and
WHEREAS, subdivision 1 of section 375 of the Vehicle and Traffic law prohibits
(i) the use or placing of posters or stickers on windshields or rear windows of motor
vehicles other than those authorized by the New York State Commissioner of Motor
Vehicles and (ii) the attaching to windshields and windshield wipers of handbills and
other forms of advertisements; and
WHEREAS, chapter 231 of the laws of 2003 provides that "[i]n a city of one
million or more, there shall be a rebuttable presumption that the person whose name,
telephone number, or other identifying information appears on any handbill or other form
of advertisement attached to a windshield or windshield wipers of a motor vehicle shall
be in violation of the provisions of subdivision 1 of section 375 of the Vehicle and Traffic
Law "relating to the prohibition against attaching to windshields and windshield wipers,
handbills and other forms of advertisements"; and
WHEREAS, chapter 231 of the laws of 2003 further provides that in addition to
those persons otherwise authorized to enforce subdivision 1 of section 375 of the Vehicle
and Traffic Law, the provisions of subdivision 1 of section 375 of the Vehicle and Traffic
Law "shall also be enforceable in a city having a population of one million or more by an
agency or agencies designated for such purpose by the mayor of such city";
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:

Section 1. The Police Department and the Department of Sanitation are
designated to enforce the provisions of subdivision 1 of section 375 of the Vehicle and
Traffic Law.
§ 2. This Order shall take effect immediately.
