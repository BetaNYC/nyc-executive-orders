EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 42

SEPTEMBER 29, 2003

REVOCATION OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 28, DATED MARCH 14, 1996, RELATING
TO MAYOR’S COMMISSION ON YOUTH EMPOWERMENT SERVICES

By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby ordered:

Section 1. Executive Order No. 28, dated March 14, 1996, is hereby revoked.

§ 2. This Order shall take effect immediately.
