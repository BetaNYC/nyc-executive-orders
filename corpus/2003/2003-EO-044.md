EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 44

NOVEMBER 17, 2003

AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 43 RELATING TO COMMISSION ON
LATIN MEDIA AND ENTERTAINMENT

By virtue of the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered:
Section 1. Section One of Executive Order No. 43, dated October 28, 2003, is amended to
read as follows:
Section 1. There is hereby established the Commission on Latin Media and Entertainment
(hereinafter called “the Commission”), under the direction of the Office of the Mayor. The
Commission shall consist of at least one Honorary Chair, two Co-Chairs, a Mayoral
Advisor/Liaison to the Commission, and at least twenty-two other members, all of whom shall be
appointed by and serve at the pleasure of the Mayor, as well as ex officio members as specified
herein. The Deputy Mayor for Economic Development and Rebuilding, the Deputy Mayor for
Legal Affairs, the Commissioner of the Mayor’s Office of Film, Theater, and Broadcasting, the
President and CEO of NYC & Company, the Executive Director of NYC Big Events, the
President of the New York City Economic Development Corporation, the Commissioner of the
Department of Small Business Services, the Commissioner of the New York city Sports
Commission, and the New York City Chief Marketing Officer shall serve as ex officio members.
§2. Effective Date. This order shall take effect immediately.
