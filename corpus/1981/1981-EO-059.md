INDUSTRIAL LOFT ADVISORY COUNCIL
ys
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of
New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Council Established.
The Industrial
Loft Advisory Council (the "Council") is hereby established
in the Office of the Mayor.
§2.
Members.
(a)
The Council shall be composed of not
less than 11 and not more than 15 members.
The
Commissioner of the Office of Economic Development
(the "Commissioner") shall be a member of the
Council, but shall not be entitled to vote.
The
other members of the Council shall be appointed by
the Mayor.
(b)
No member of the Council shall reside in
a loft,
(c)
Members of the Council shall serve for
a term of two years.
A vacancy other than by expiration of a term shall be filled by the Mayor
for the remainder of the term.
(ad)
The Chairman of the Council shall be
designated by the Mayor from among the voting
members.
§3.
Purposes.
The Council may advise the City
Planning Commission and the Board of Standards and Appeals
with respect to all applications concerning the conversion
to residential use of loft, factory and warehouse buildings
54

located in the City of New York and currently used for commercial, manufacturing, wholesaling, warehousing and related
activities.
The Council may also advise these bodies and
the Board of Estimate concerning the effect which granting
or denying such applications would have on the retention
of industry within the City and may take such other actions
with respect to such applications as may be authorized by
the Zoning Resolution.
§4.
Action by the Council
(a)
The Council shall meet at the call of
yehe Chairman.
(b)
The presence of a majority of the
voting members shall constitute a quorom.
(c)
The Council shall act by the affirmative vote of a majority of those members present
and voting.
§5.
Staff and Expenses.
Members of the council
shall serve without compensation.
No expense shall be incurred by the Council for personnel, equipment, or otherwise,
except that, subject to the approval of the Commissioner,
personnel of the Office of Economic Development may provide
the Council with staff assistance.
§6.
Effective Date.
This Executive Order shall
take effect immediately.
