OFFICE OF RAIL FREIGHT DEVELOPMENT
by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of
New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Office Established.
The Office of
Rail Freight Development (the "Office”)
is hereby established
in the Office of the Mayor.
The Office shall be headed by a
Director who shall report to the Deputy Mayor for Economic
Policy and Development.
§2.
Purposes of the Office.
(a)
Policy Development and Coordination.
The Office shall have overall responsibility
for the development and coordination of rail freight
policy for the City of New York.
Agencies engaged in
activities affecting rail freight transportation
shall consult with the Office for the purpose of improving rail freight service within the City.
(b)
Capital Improvement.
After consultation
with the Department of Transportation, the Office
shall develop a capital improvement program for
the following purposes:
(i)
to rehabilitate obsolete, abandoned
or deteriorated terminals, yard facilities,
tracks and related equipment;
(ii)
to remove clearance restrictions
which block access to the City by rail
freight rolling stock;
(iii) to achieve rapid, convenient and
cost-effective rail freight service to businesses within the City; and

(iv)
to make such other improvements
as may further the objectives of the City
rail freight policy.
(c)
Port Development.
After consultation
with the Department of Ports and Terminals, the
Office shall develop a program to increase the
economic competitiveness of the combined rail
and freight transportation facilities of the
Port of New York.
(d)
Government and Industry Liaison.
‘The
Office shall monitor federal, state and local
Policy developments concerning rail freight
transportation and shall make appropriate recom~
mendations to the Deputy Mayor for Economic
Policy and Development with respect thereto.
The Office shall maintain liaison with federal,
state and local officials and with regional
rail freight carriers, shippers, receivers and
consumers.
§3.
Staff and Expenses.
The Office shall have
such staff and shail incur such expenses as provided in the
budget of the Office of the Mayor.
§4.
Effective Date.
This Executive Order shall
take effect immediately.
