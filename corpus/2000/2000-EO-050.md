EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 50
NOVEMBER 1, 2000
ESTABLISHMENT OF CHARTER SCHOOL IMPROVEMENT FUND COMMITTEE
WHEREAS, the development and encouragement of charter schools is an important
component of efforts to improve the quality of education in New York City, and
WHEREAS, funding has been allocated in the Fiscal Year “01 budget for educational
reform strategies and innovations, including the support of charter schools through contributions
to capital related and start-up costs of such schools, and
WHEREAS, such funds should be distributed to charter schools that can demonstrate
effective and appropriate use of such funds; and
WHEREAS, a committee charged with coordination and oversight of the distribution of
such funds, having as its members the Commissioner of the Department of Youth and
Community Development, the Commissioner of the Department of Design and Construction and
the Director of the Office of Management and Budget, can best ensure the success of a charter
school grant program;
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor 0 f the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:
Section 1. Establishment of Charter School Improvement lund Committee. There is
hereby established a City of New York Charter School Improvement Fund Committee (“the
Committee”) reporting to the Mayor, through the Deputy Mayor for Planning, Education and
Cultural Affairs, The Committee shall consist of the Commissioner of the Department of Youth
and Community Development, who shall serve as Chair, the Commissioner of the Department of
Design and Construction and the Director of the Office of Management and Budget, or their
designees.
Section 2. Functions of the Committee. The Committee shall:
a. Solicit applications from, and at its discretion award grants to, charter schools
located in the City of New York for specific capital and start-up projects.

Section 3. Agency Assistance. All City agencies shall cooperate in providing to the
Committee such personnel, facilities, information and other assistance as is necessary and
required by the Committee to carry out its responsibilities.
Section 4. Effective Date. This order shall take effect immediately.
