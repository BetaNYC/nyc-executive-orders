EXECUTIVE ORDER NO.65
JULY 27, 1976
INTERAGENCY COMMITTEE ON MINORITY BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT
Whereas, There has been established within the Economic Development Administration of The City of New York an office of Minority Business Enterprise; and
Whereas, A major objective of that office is to involve City government in the stimulation and promotion of minority business development; and
.
Whereas, It is important that City agencies engaging in the procurement of goods
and services for The City of New York coordinate their efforts to achieve greater participation by minority businesses.
:
ow, therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York, it is
hereby ordered as follows:
Section 1.
There is hereby established an Interagency Committee on Minority Business Development.
$2.
The Committee shall consist of the Economic Development Administrator, Commissioner of Purchase or their duly designated representatives and policy level officials
