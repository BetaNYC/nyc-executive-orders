EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 72
SEPTEMBER 16, 1976
ESTABLISHMENT OF A DEPARTMENT OF PARKS AND
RECREATION CONCESSIONS’ REVIEW COMMITTEE
”
Whereas, The City of New York recognizes the need to continue to provide various
services to the residents of the City in its parks and public recreation areas; and
Whereas, {t is desirable that certain of these public services be operated under contract
with the City by persons and corporations
qualified to operate such services; and
Whereas, It is in the best interest of
The City of New York and its residents that
such services be delivered to the residents of the City in the best possible manner; and
Whereas, Revenues are derived by the City
from the sale of services to the public
by such persons and corporations and it is desirable that the revenues be as large as possible,
consistent with proper park use; and
. Whereas, It is to the best interest of the City that the Department of Parks and
Recreation solicit proposals for future concessions.
Now, therefore,
by virtue of the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New
York, it ig hereby ordered as follows :
Section J.
There is established a Committee for the review of Department of
Parks and Recreation concession agreements. This Committee shall consist of the
Commissioner of Parks and Recreation, who shall be the Chairman, the Director of
Management and Budget, and the Corporation Counsel. The Comptroller shall also
be invited to serve as a member of the Committee.
This Committee shall have the following functions, powers and duties:
(a) To recommend approval or disapproval of each future proposed concession
license which is expected to have estimated gross annual receipts in excess of seventyfive thousand dollars ;
.
.
(b) To review and evaluate any other present or future concession agreements
regardless of the estimated gross receipts, wherein the Commigsioner of
Parks and
Recreation shall determine that such solicitation for proposals is consistent with
the City’s desire to maximize potential revenues.
§3,
This Order shall take effect immediately.
