Each agency is to submit to the Board of Estimate prompt notification of all
surrenders of improved or unimproved properties as soon as such properties are no
longer required for agency functions.

Releasing Space to the Department of Real Estate
Each agency is to submit to the Commissioner of Real Estate prompt notification
of all releases of City-owned or leased space as soon as such space is no longer
required for agency functions.

(21) month period. The reports are to be submitted not later than April 1 and
