EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 67
AUGUST 16, 1976
AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO, &
DATED JULY 1, 1968
Whereas, It is necessary and desirable to amend Executive Order No. 84, dated July
1, 1968, as amended by Executive Order No. 34, dated August 26, 1975, regarding the
New York City Council Against Poverty.
Now, therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York,
it is hereby ordered as follows:
:
Section 1.
Section 1, subsection d of Executive Order No. 84, dated July 1, 1968
as pended by Executive Order No. #4, dated August 26, 1975, is hereby amended to read
as follows:
“(d) Term.
The term of office for members of the Council other than public
officiats shall be five years. Public officials may serve unlimited terms. All other members shall not hold office beyond five consecutive years or a total of ten years.”
§2.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
a20-27
ABRAHAM D, BEAME, Mayor.
