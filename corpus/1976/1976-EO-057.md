OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 57
APRIL 12, 1976
AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 62
DATED FEBRUARY 5, 1968
;
AS AMENDED BY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 56
DATED FEBRUARY 1, 1972
Whereas, The Urban Design Council created by Executive Order No. 62, dated
February 5, 1968, as amended
Executive Order No. 56, dated February 1, 1972, has
recommended certain changes in the composition of the Council; and
Whereas, It is necessary and desirable to amend said Executive Order.
Now, therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York, it is
hereby ordered as follows:
Section 1.
Sections 3 and 5 of Executive Order No, 62, dated February 5, 1968, as
amended by Executive Order No, 56, dated February 1, 1972, are hereby amended to read
as
follows
:
“83,
Composition.
The Urban Design
Council shall consist of not less than ten
nor more than twenty individuals. They shall be appointed by the Mayor, with due
;
consideration for their understanding
of the probl
of urban design and their capacity
for giving clear, effective advice. They shal! serve at the pleasure of the Mayor and
without compensation.”
§5.
Procedure, Alt actions of the Urban Design Council shall be by
a majorit
of those present, with a quorum of six required. In other respects, the
Council shall
establish its own rules of procedure, provided it shall meet at feast once a month, considering July and August a single month.”
§2. This Order shail take effect immediately.
