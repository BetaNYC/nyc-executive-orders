AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 2
DATED JANUARY 1, 1974.
Whereas, It is necessary and desirable to amend Executive Order No. 2 dated January
1, 1974, as amended by Executive Order No. 40 dated September 25, 1975, so as to designate

‘a Deputy Mayor for

hereby ordered as follows;
|
Section 1.
Paragraph 1 of Executive Order No. 2 dated January 1, 1974 as amended
by Executive Order No. 40 dated September 25, 1975, is hereby amended as follows:
M
“he There shall be five Deputy Mayors, one of whom shall be designated First Deputy
ayor.
§2.
There shall be a new paragraph added to Executive Order No. 2 dated January 1,
1974 as amended by Executive Order No. 40, dated September 25, 1975 which shall be numbered paragraph
6 thereof. It shall read as follows :
“6.
One
Deputy Mayor shall—
(a) Be a Deputy Mayor for Economic Development who shall head the economic development program and coordinate the efforts to foster the economic growth of the City.
(b) In the absence of the Mayor from sessions of the Board of Estimate, act on behalf
of the Mayor at such sessions of the Board of Estimate and represent the Mayor when so
directed by the Mayor on any board, committee, commission or corporation of which the
Mayor is a member.
(c) Consult with and advise the Mayor on matters concerning economic development
and
perform such other duties as the Mayor may direct.”
§3, Former paragraph 6 of Executive Order No. 2 dated January 1, 1974 as amended
by Executive Order No. 40 dated September 25, 1975 shall be renumbered paragraph 7
thereof. It shall read as follows :
“7.

15, 1970, Executive Order No. 23 dated August 24, 1970, Executive Order No. 31 dated
January 18, 1971 and Executive Order No. 74 dated January 23, 1973 are amended so as to
delete therefrom references to Deputy Mayor-City Administrator and City Administrator.”
