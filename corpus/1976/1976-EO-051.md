OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
——
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 51
FEBRUARY 4, 1976
ESTABLISHMENT OF PROCEDURES FOR CENTRALIZED DIRECTION
OF TITLE X ACTIVITIES.
Whereas, Federal funds are granted to various agencies under Title X of the Public
Works and Economic Development Act of 1965, as amended, for the purposes of relieving
unemployment by providing job
opportunities : and
hereas, It is necessary that the City be enabled to alleviate losses of vital public
services, and properly utilize available funds to meet City needs; and
Whereas, These objectives can best be implemented by providing for centralized
direction of Title X activities and direct reporting to the Office of the Mayor
Now, therefore, under the powers vested in me by law, it is hereby ordered that;
Section 1,
Title X Coordinator.
All Title X activities shall be coordinated by the Commissioner of the Department of
Employment of the Human Resources Administration who shall report directly to the
First Deputy Mayor.
