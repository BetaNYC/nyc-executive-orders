EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 92

TERMINATION OF A STATE OF EMERGENCY

July 27, 2006

Section 1. Pursuant to the powers vested in me by laws of the State of New York and the
City of New York, including but not limited to Executive Law § 24, I hereby terminate the State
of Emergency regarding a power outage in Queens declared in Executive Order 91 on July 24,
2006. Executive Order 91 continued Executive Order 90 which originally declared the State of
Emergency on July 19, 2006.

§ 2.
This Order shall take effect immediately.

_____________________________
Michael R. Bloomberg
Mayor
