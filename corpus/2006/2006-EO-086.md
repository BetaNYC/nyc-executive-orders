EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 86
March 2, 2006
ACQUISITION OF PROPERTY, AND GRANTS OF CONSERVATION EASEMENTS,
FOR WATER SUPPLY PURPOSES

By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1. Implementation of Mayoral Duties in connection with the Acquisition of Real
Property Outside the City of New York for Water Supply Purposes. The Director of the Office
of Operations, and staff of such Office designated by such Director, are hereby authorized to
give preliminary approval to the acquisition of real property outside the City of New York for
water supply purposes pursuant to section 5-357 of the Administrative Code.
§ 2. Approval of Maps and Payments. The Deputy Mayor for Economic Development
and Rebuilding is hereby authorized to sign the certificate of final adoption of acquisition maps
pursuant to section 5-360 of the Administrative Code, and to approve amounts to be paid for the
real property identified on such maps pursuant to section 5-361 of the Administrative Code.
§ 3. Execution of Easement Deeds. The Deputy Mayor for Economic Development and
Rebuilding is hereby authorized to execute deeds and other legal instruments necessary to grant
easements for the protection of the municipal water supply pursuant to section 4-106(9) of the
Administrative Code and section 384 of the Charter.
§ 4. Mayoral Hearings on Water Supply Property Acquisitions and the Disposition of
Easements. The Director of the Mayor’s Office of Contracts, and staff of such Office designated
by such Director, are hereby authorized to arrange and administer any public hearings, including
required public notice thereof, to be held in connection with the acquisition of real property
outside of the City of New York for water supply purposes pursuant to section 5-358 of the
Administrative Code, and in connection with the granting of easements for the protection of the
municipal water supply pursuant to section 4-106(9) of the Administrative Code and section 384
of the Charter.

§ 5. Executive Order No. 37, dated July 28, 1997, as amended by Executive Order No.
32, dated January 6, 2003, is hereby REPEALED.
§ 6. This Order shall take effect immediately
______________________
Michael R. Bloomberg
Mayor
