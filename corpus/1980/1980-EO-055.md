EXECUTIVE ORDER NO.
55
December 9, 1980
RAPID TRANSIT LAW; DELEGATION OF AUTHORITY
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City
of New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
The authority granted to the Mayor
by Rapid Transit Law, Section 34(b) is hereby delegated to
the Director of Management and Budget.
§2.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
Edward I. Koch
