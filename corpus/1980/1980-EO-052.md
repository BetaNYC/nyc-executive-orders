Efeuidive Ghar
OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
NEW YORK, 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO.
52
JUNE 26, 1980
DEPUTY MAYORS
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City
of New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Deputy Mayor for Policy.
Paragraph
(c) of Section 4 of Executive Order Number 4] (1980) is
hereby amended to read as follows:
"§4.
The Deputy Mayor for Policy shall:
"(c)
succeed to the powers and
duties of Health Services Administrator, as specified in Chapter
1016
of the Laws of 1969
(Section
7381, et seg. of the Unconsolidated
Laws of New York).
§2.
Prior Order Revoked.
Executive Order
Number 38 (1979)
is hereby revoked.
§3.
Effective Date.
This Order shall take
effect immediately.
