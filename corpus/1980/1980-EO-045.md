Act for, on behalf of and at the direction of
the Mayor in the exercise of all functions, powers and duties
which the Mayor may have pursuant to the New York City Loan
Guarantee Act of 1978, P. L.
95~339, as the same may be from

an Agreement to Guarantee by and among

November 15,

between The City of New York and certain banks,
and any other
seasonal financing agreement of The City of New York, including,
but not limited to, full power to execute for, on behalf of,
and
at the direction of the Mayor, any document pursuant to any such
agreement and any agreement, amendment to such agreement or other
document pursuant to such law.

and duties for, on behalf of, and at the direction of the Mayor
by such Deputy Mayor pursuant to this delegation shall not require
