OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
NEw YorRK, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO.
117
August 16, 1988
Revocation of Executive Order No. 20 (Dated
September 7, 1978), Establishment of the Community
Development Committee to Coordinate the Community
Development Block Grant Program.
By Virtue of the power vested in me as Mayor of the
City of New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Prior Order Revoked.
Executive Order No.
20, dated September 7, 1978, is hereby revoked.
Section 2.
Effective Date.
This Order shall take
effect immediately.
Edward I. Kach
MAYOR
