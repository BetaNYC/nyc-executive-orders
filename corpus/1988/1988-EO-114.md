OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
New Yorn, N.Y. 10007
Executive Order No. 114
April 13, 1988
OFFICE OF CONTRACTS
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1. Office Established. The Office of Contracts
(the "Office") is hereby established in the Office of the
Mayor.
The Office shall be headed by a Director who shall
report to the First Deputy Mayor.
Section 2. Purposes. The Office of Contracts will
perform the following functions:
a)
Draft guidelines and policy statements for review
and issuance by the Mayor.
b)
Inform the Mayor of major policy issues or
patterns that emerge in City contracting.
c)
Provide technical assistance on contract related
issues to line departments.
d)
Maintain liaison with the departmental chief
contracting officers.
e)
Review departmental and specialized contract
guidelines and procedures for conformance with
citywide guidelines and regulations.
£)
Conduct post-audit reviews of selected contracts
to ensure adherence to proper procedures.
g)
Coordinate citywide information on contracts and
contractors.

h)
Establish working relationships with associations
of contractors from industry and nonprofit groups
that do business with the City in order to make
working for the City attractive to the best
sources available.
Section 3. Staff and Expenses.
The Office shall have
such staff and incur such expenses necessary for it to
perform its responsibilities and effectuate its purposes.
Section 4. Effective Date.
This Order shall take
effect immediately.
Edward I. Koch
—
MAYOR
