DELEGATION OF AUTHORITY TO DEFUTY MAYOR FOR FINANCE
Whereas, The City of New York may from time to time enter into agreements with
the Municipal Assistance Corporation for The City of New York; and
Whereas, Such agreements are beneficial and necessary to the financial stability of
The City of New York; and
;
Whereas, Kenneth S$. Axelson, Deputy Mayor for Finance of The City of New York,
is fully familiar with the facts necessary to entering into such agreements.
Now, therefore, by virtue of the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New
work and pursuant to section three of the New York City Charter, it is hereby ordered as
ollows :
Section 1.
Kenneth S. Axelson, Deputy Mayor for Finance of The City of New
York, is hereby delegated the authority to act for, on behalf of, and at the direction of
the Mayor ef The City of New York in the exercise of all functions, powers and duties
which the Mayor of The City of New York may have pursuant to Article 10 of the
Public Authorities Law as enacted by Laws of 1975, chapters 168 and 169 (the “Act"),
as the same may be amended from time to time, including but limited to, full power to
execute for, on behalf of, and at the direction of the Mayor of The City of New York:
any agreement pursuant to section 3038 of the Act between The City of New York and
the Municipal Assistance Corporation for The City of New York, any certificate pursuant
to section 3037 of the Act, and any cther document in connection with the Act,
§2.
The exercise of functions, powers and duties for, on behalf of, and at the direetion of the Mayor of The City of New York by the Deputy Mayor for Finance of The
City of New York pursuant to the delegation established by this Executive Order shall
not require the consent in writing of the Mayor or any other Deputy Mayor of The City
of New York.

For the purposes of Article 2, section 9 of the Public Officers Law, as enacted
by Laws of 1909, chapter 51, notwithstanding the temporary inability of the Mayor of
