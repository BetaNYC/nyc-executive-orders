OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO, 34
AUGUST 26, 1975
AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 84
(Dated July 1, 1968)
Whereas, It is necessary and desirable to amend Executive Order No, 84, dated July 1,
1968, regarding the New Vork City Council Against Poverty.
Now, therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York, it is
hereby ordered as follows:
Section 1.
Section 1, subsection d of Executive Order No. 84, dated July 1, 1968, is
hereby amended to read as follows:
erm.
The term of office for members of the Council other than public
officials shalt he four years, Public officiats may serve unlimited terms. All other members shall not hold office beyond four consecutive years or a total of eight years.”
§2. This Order shalt take effect immediately.
a30-s6
ABRAHAM D. BEAME, Mayor.
