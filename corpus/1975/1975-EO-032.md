OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 32
JULY 15, 1975
MONITORING OF THE EXPENDITURES OF CITY AGENCIES
Whereas, The City of New York must make every effort to insure the economic and
efficient operation of all City agencies; and
_ Whereas, it is the clear policy of this administration to operate its Mayoral agencies
strictly within budgeted revenues; and
Whereas, It is necessary that the non-Mayoral agencies function within the allotments
of tax levy funds that are currently available.
:
Now, Therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York, it is
hereby ordered :
Section 1.
The Director of the Office of Management and Budget is hereby ordered
to take all necessary steps to closely monitor expenditures of all agencies to insure that expenses remain strictly within available revenues.
.
The non-Mayoral agencies, such as the Health and Hospitals Corporation, Board
of Education and Board of Higher Education are hereby notified that their allotments
represent the maximum: tax levy funds that are available within the current 1975-76 budget.
§3. All City agencies must remain within their current allotments and not permit the
creation of future deficits by any action during the 1975-76 fiscal year.
§4.
All City agencies are hereby notified that they must immediately review their
payrolls and make any necessary adjustments to insure that they will operate within their
budgetary allocations in the 1975-76 fiscal year.
$5.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
ABRAHAM D. BEAME, Mayor.
