OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 35
AUGUST 28, 1975
ELIMINATION OF THE OFFICE OF STATEN ISLAND DEVELOPMENT
hereas, The City of New York is currently in a budgetary crisis which calls for
the curtailment of certain services ; and
hereas, It is necessary to make critical choices in response to the present fiscal
situation; and
hereas, Arrangements can be made for the functions of the Office of Staten Island
Development to be absorbed by other City agencies.
Now, therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered as follows:
Section {.
Executive Order No. 27, dated August 3, 1966, establishing the Office
of Staten Island Development is hereby repealed.
§2.
This Order shall take effect on September 13, 1975.
s4-10
ABRAHAM D. BEAME, Mayor.
