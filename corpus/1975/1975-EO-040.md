OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
‘EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 40
SEPTEMBER 25, 1975
AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 2 DATED JANUARY I, 1974
Whereas, It is necessary and desirable to amend Executive Order No. 2 dated January 1, 1974, so as to designate a Deputy Mayor for Finance.
Now, therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York,
pursuant to the
provisions of sections three, seven and subdivision b of section eleven hundred two of the
New York City Charter, it is hereby ordered as follows:
Section |.
Paragraph 1 of Executive Order No. 2 dated January 1, 1974, is hereby
amended as follows:
‘l.
There shall be four Deputy Mayors, one of whom shall be designated First
Deputy Mayor.”
2.
There shall be a new
paragraph added to Executive Order No. 2 dated January
1, 1974, which shall be numbered paragraph 5 thereof. It shall read as follows:
“§.
One Deputy Mayor shall
(a) Be a
Deputy
Mayor for Finance and supervise the City’s fiscal operations
and affairs.
.
(8) Act on behalf of and at the direction of the Mayor in exercising and carrying out functions, powers and duties vested in or imposed upon the Mayor by the
New York State Municipal Assistance Corporation Act, the Municipal Assistance
Corporation for The City of New York Act and the New York
State Financial
Emergency Act for The City of New York.
(c) In the absence of the Mayor from sessions of the Board of Estimate act
on behalf of the Mayor at such sessions of the Board of Estimate and represent
the Mayor when so directed by the Mayor on any board, committee, commission
or corporation of which the Mayor is a member.
(8) Consult with and advise the Mayor on matters of City fiscal policy and
perform such other duties as the Mayor may direct.”
§3.
Former paragraph 5 of Executive Order No. 2 dated January 1, 1974, shall be
renumbered paragraph 6 thereof, It shall read as follows:
“6.
Executive Order No. 71 dated April 2, 1968, Executive Order No. 20 dated
July 15, 1970, Executive Order No. 23 dated August 24, 1970, Executive Order No.
31 dated January 18, 1971 and Executive Order No. 74 dated January 23, 1973 are
amended so as to delete therefrom references to Deputy Mayor-City Administrator
and City Administrator.”
§4.
Former paragraph 6 of Executive Order No. 2 dated January 1, 1974, shall be
renumbered paragraph 7
thereof. It shall read as follows:
“7.
Executive Order No. 9 dated March 4, 1966 and Executive Order No. 4
“4. ‘dated January 28, 1970 are hereby rescinded.”
85. This order shall take effect immediately.
vas
ABRAHAM D. BEAME, Mayor.”
