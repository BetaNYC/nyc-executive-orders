Whereas, The City recognizes the need to review existing youth services and create
a more effective mechanism to maximize use of existing resources and to identify new
resources.
Now, therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York, it is
hereby ordered :
ection 1,
There shall be a Youth Board which shall continue as part of the Office
of the Mayor and be directly responsible to the Chief Executive.
§2.
The reorganized Youth Board shall be responsible for the development of the
City’s general policies and goals for the prevention and reduction of juvenile delinquency
and improvement of youth service delivery by acting as the Mayor's policy recommending
body for the guidance of those public or private or religious youth related agencies or divisions of agencies offering essential youth services utilizing public funds.
§3.
The Youth Board shall have the following powers, duties and responsibilities :
(a) Obtain, assemble and develop statistical records and data, including those
that reflect the incidence and trends of delinquency and youth crimes and offenses
in the City. Make studies, conduct public forums and hearings and develop recommendations to meet the needs of the City’s youth ;
(b) Collect, correlate and disseminate information regarding existing youth services to all youth related agencies and to the community at large;
{c) Evaluate the performance of youth service agencies, determine service gaps,
recommend priorties and submit recommendations to the Mayor to reorganize, restructure, integrate or eliminate those agencies to improve service delivery;
(d) Participate in the preparation of comprehensive plans pertaining in whole
or in part to youth development and the prevention and reduction of juvenile delinquency;
(¢) Encourage joint efforts among major youth agencies to address and seek
resolution of common youth related problems ;
(£) In conjunction with the Office of Management and Budget, establish priorities
and recommend the allocation of funds for youth programs and further recommend
methods of assuring efficient fiscal administration of
such funds;

agencies in obtaining those funds ;
