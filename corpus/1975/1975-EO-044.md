OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 44
OCTOBER 31, 1975
ESTABLISHMENT OF A MUNICIPAL NEWS AND
INFORMATION BUREAU IN THE OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
Whereas, It is necessary for the City to provide fast and accurate information on the
functions of its various agencies to the media and the public: and
:
Whereas, The fiscal constraints of the City demand that these services be performed
efficiently and economically ;
Now, therefore, pursuant to the authority vested in me as Mayor of The City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
There is hereby created a Municipal News and Information Bureau in the
Office of the Mayor.
\
§2.
The
Municipal News and Information Bureau will have the following duties:
(a) to act as public information representative for Mayoral agencies not staffed
with public affairs or public information personnel and to provide all duties normally
associated with that function.
