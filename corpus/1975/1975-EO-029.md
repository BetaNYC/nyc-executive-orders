OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 29
APRIL 16, 1975
MUNICIPAL ENERGY CONSERVATION PROGRAM
Whereas, Pursuant to the New York City Charter, the Municipal Service Administration shaff have charge and control over regulation and the use of energy throughout City
government.
Whereas, Pursuant to the above-mentioned charter, the Municipal Service Adiministration shall, together with the Director of Management and Budget, establish the New
York City government’s overall policy with regard to energy conservation in all municipal
buildings and facilities.
Now, therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York, it is
hereby ordered as follows :
Section 1.
A Municipal Energy Conservation Program is hereby established in the
Municipal Service Administration to control and regulate the use of energy in all agencies
where City funds are appropriated for the payment of such costs.
§2.
The Municipal Energy Conservation Program shall have a Coordinator who shall
be designated by the Municipal Service Administrator and the Director of Management
and Budget.
