EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 82
ocToBER 4,2021
REVOCATION OF BAN ON NON-ESSENTIAL FOREIGN TRAVEL IN RESPONSE TO
THE CORONAVIRUS
WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 55 prohibited employees of City agencies from non-essential
foreign travel for official City business, in response to the COVID-19 pandemic; and
WHEREAS, this ban on non-essential foreign travel is no longer necessary;
NOW, THEREFORE, pursuant to the powers vested in me by the laws of the State of New York
and the City of New York:
Section 1. I hereby revoke Executive order No. 55, dated March 9,2020.
$ 2. This order shall take effect immediately.
