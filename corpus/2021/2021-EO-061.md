EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 61
DESIGNATION OF THE OFFICE OF LONG TERM PLANNING AND SUSTAINABILITY
AS THE RULEMAKING ENTITY FOR THE DESIGNATION OF PRIORITY DISTRICTS
RECEIVING THE ENHANCED GREEN ROOF TAX ABATEMENT.
JANUARY 22,2021
WHEREAS, the New York State Assembly amended section 499-aaa of the New York
State Real Property Tax Law in2019, updating and extending the NYC green roof tax abatement
and increasing the tax abatement from $5.23 per square foot to $15 per square foot of green roof
in priority districts;
WHEREAS, the law requires that an agency designated by the mayor of the city of New
York select the priority districts receiving the enhanced abatement;
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:
Section 1. The Office of Long Term Planning and Sustainability is designated as the
administering entity for designating priority districts for the Green Roof Tax Abatement, provided
that the Office of Long Term Planning and Sustainability select the priority districts consistent
with the standards and requirements outlined in the Real Property Tax Law. The Office of Long
Term Plaruring and Sustainability shall prioritize areas in the priority combined sewage overflow
tributary areas, with particular emphasis on those portions of the city that have been identified as
lacking green space.
$ 2. This Order shall take effect immediately.
