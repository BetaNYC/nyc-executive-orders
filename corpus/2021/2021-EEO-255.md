NOW, THEREFORE, pursuant to the powers vested in me by the laws of the State ofNew

Section 1. I hereby direct that sections 1 and 2 of Emergency Executive Order No. 251,
dated September 29,2021, are extended for five (5) days.
$ 2. I hereby direct the Fire and Police Departments, the Department of Buildings, the
Sheriff, and other agencies as needed, to enforce the directives set forth in this Order in accordance
with their lawful authorities, including Administrative Code sections 15-227(a),28-105.10.1, and
28-201.1, and section 107.6 of the Fire Code. Violations of the directives set forth inthis Order
may be issued as if they were violations under the Health Code sections 3.07 and 3.11, and
enforced by the Department of Health and Mental Hygiene or any other agency.
$ 3. This Emergency Executive Order shall take effect immediately.
