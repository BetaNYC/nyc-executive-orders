THE CITY OF NEW YORK
OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
NE.w YORK, N . Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 40
January 22, 2019
CONNECTING NEW YORKERS TO HEAL TH INSURANCE
WHEREAS, of the estimated 600,000 New Yorkers who remain without health insurance, an
estimated half are eligible to enroll in a health insurance plan; and
WHEREAS, despite the City's concerted efforts to infonn and enroll eligible New Yorkers to this
point, some New Yorkers are still without infonnation regarding their eligibility;
WHEREAS, it is necessary to further coordinate the City's range of efforts to facilitate the
enrollment of eligible uninsured individuals;
WHEREAS, New York City's MetroPlus is an existing public option for all New Yorkers;
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered:
Section l. Coordination of Citv Efforts. The Mayor's Public Engagement Unit (PEU) shall, in
consultation with the Deputy Mayor for Health and Human Services, coordinate efforts by City agencies
to provide infonnation about health insurance and enrollment assistance to individuals who use City
services. To assist agencies in fulfilling this mandate, PEU shall establish guidelines for agencies and
provide them with resources for communicating with uninsured and a referral process for New York City
residents who are uninsured to be connected to enrollment assistance. These resources shall include, but
not be limited to, infonnation about MetroPlus.
§ 2. Agency Cooperation. City agencies, including all City agencies providing direct services to
New Yorkers, shall cooperate with these coordination efforts as required.
§ 3. Reporting. PEU shall develop a system for agencies not already reporting on enrollment
efforts to do so.
§ 4. Effective Date. This order shall take effect immediately.
