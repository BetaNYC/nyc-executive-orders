BUSINESS EMERGENCY TASK FORCE
(Amendment of Executive Order No. 54, dated March 5, 1993)
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered as follows:
Section 1. Priot Order Amended. Subdivision (a) of Section 3 of Executive Order
No. 54, dated March 5, 1993, is amended to read as follows:
a.
The following City officers shall also be members of the Task Force:
(1)
the Commissioner of Business Services or his or her designee;
(2)
the Commissioner of Buildings or his or her designee;
(3)
the Police Commissioner or his or her designee;
(4)
the Fire Commissioner or his or her designee; and
(5)
the Commissioner of Telecommunications and
- Energy or his or her designee;
Any person designated to sit on the Task Force pursuant to this subdivision shall be
a senior official or staff member having experience in and familiarity with matters pertaining
to emergency response.
Section 2.
Effective Date. This Order shall take effect immediately.
