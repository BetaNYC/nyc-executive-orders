THE City oF NEw YorK
OFFICE OF THE Mayor
NEW YorK, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO.
47
January 4, 1993
OFFICE OF FILM, THEATRE AND
BROADCASTING
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Continuation of Office of Film, Theatre and
Broadcasting.
There is continued within the Office of the Mayor an
Office
of
Film,
Theatre
and Broadcasting,
to
be
headed
by
a
commissioner who shall be appointed by and serve at the pleasure of
the Mayor.
The
commissioner shall report to
and
be directly
accountable
to
the
Deputy
Mayor
for
Finance
and
Economic
Development.
§ 2.
Scope of Duties.
The Office of Film, Theatre and
Broadcasting shall:
a.
Issue all permits required for film and television
production and for still photography shoots in the City.
b.
Provide information
and assistance necessary to
enable filmmakers and broadcasters to operate in the City.
Cc.
Develop
programs
and
activities
designed
to
encourage theatrical production and attendance in the City.
d.
Perform
such
other
functions
as
are
deemed
necessary by the
commissioner
to facilitate film,
theater
and
television production in the City.

§ 3.
Cooperation and Assistance.
The heads of all City
departments
and agencies,
including the Police Department,
the
Department
of
Parks
and
Recreation
and
the
Department
of
Transportation, shall cooperate with and assist the Office of Filn,
Theatre and Broadcasting in carrying out its responsibilities under
this Order.
§ 4.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
