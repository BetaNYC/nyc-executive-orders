EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 30

INTERAGENCY TASK FORCE ON SMALL BUSINESS
WHEREAS,
the economic well-being of
New York City is
dependent, in part, upon the City's ability to respond to the needs
of its businesses and industries; and
WHEREAS, representatives of City agencies involved in the
regulation
of and delivery of municipal services to small businesses
must be able to communicate quickly and effectively with one another
in order to ensure productive interaction between the City aii the
small business community;
NOW THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor. of
the City of New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section
1.
Creation
of
Task Force.
There
is
hereby
established within the office of the Deputy Mayor for Finance and
Economic Development an Interagency Task Force on Small Business
("Task Force").
§ 2.
Functions of Task Force.
The functions and duties
of the Task Force shall include:

a.
evaluating
agency
procedures
and
operations
where
these may affect segments of New York City's
small
busiress community;
b.
reviewing
relevant
City
rules
and
procedures
and
making recommendations for changes therein designed
to promote and foster the growth of small businesses
in the City;
oS
subject to approval of the Mayor and the Deputy Mayor
for
Finance
and
Economic
Development,
making
determinations with respect to issues confronting the
City
which,
in
the
estimation
of
Task
Force
participants,
may
have
a
significant
impact
on
the
small business community; and
d.
undertaking other activities and measures as directed
by
the
Deputy
Mayor
for
Finance
and
Economic
Development to ensure productive interaction between
the City and the small business community.
