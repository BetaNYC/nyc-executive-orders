THE City oF New YorK

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 31
November
22, 1991
HAZARDOUS WASTE SITE REMEDIATION FUNDING
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Application and Other Acts Authorized.
The Commissioner of Environmental
Protection is hereby authorized to act on behalf of the City in all matters related to funding by the State
. of hazardous waste site remediation projects known as Pelham Bay, Brookfield, Pennsylvania and Fountain
Avenue Landfills, in Title 3 of Article 52 of the Environmental Conservation Law, including but not
limited to making applications, executing the State Assistance Contract, and submitting project
documentation.
Section 2. All applications and other documents heretofore signed, or other actions heretofore
taken, by the Commissioner of Sanitation with respect to funding by the State of hazardous waste site
remediation projects, known as Pelham Bay, Brookfield, Pennsylvania and Fountain Avenue Landfills,
pursuant to Title 3 of Article 52 of the Environmental Conservation Law, are hereby ratified and
continued in effect.
Section 3. This order shall take effect immediately.
