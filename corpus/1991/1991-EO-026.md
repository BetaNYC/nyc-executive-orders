THE City of New YorRK
OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
New YorRK. N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 26
AUGUST 28, 1991
CABLE TELEVISION FRANCHISES
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered as follows:
Section 1.
Pursuant to subdivision f of section 8 of the New York City
Charter, the Deputy Mayor for Finance and Economic Development is delegated all powers
and duties of the Mayor with respect to cable television franchises.
Section 2.
All communications to the Mayor concerning the aforesaid
franchises shall instead be directed to the Deputy Mayor for Finance and Economic
Development.
Section 3.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
