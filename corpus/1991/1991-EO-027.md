THE City of NEw YorRK
OFFICE OF THE Mayor
New York, N.Y. 10007
Executive Order No. _27
September 10, 1991
NEW YORK CITY COMMISSION ON HOMELESSNESS
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:
Section
1.
Creation
of
Commission.
There
is
hereby
established a
commission
to conduct a comprehensive review of the
problem of
homelessness
and
to
make
recommendations
as
to
more
efficient and effective responses to this crisis.
The members shall be
appointed by and serve at the pleasure of the Mayor.
The Mayor
shall
designate
a
chairperson
from
among
the
members
of
the
Commission so appointed.
§2.
Powers and duties.
The Commission shall undertake a
comprehensive
assessment
of
the
problem
of
homelessness,
and,
submit to the Mayor by January 31, 1992, a report setting forth its
findings and making recommendations with respect to all aspects of
homelessness, including:
a)
The prevention of homelessness;
b)
The need for a restructuring of current approaches to
providing emergency housing for homeless persons;

c)
The appropriate roles of the federal, state and
local
governments,
and the private sector, in addressing the problem of
homelessness;
d)
The efficacy of federal and
state regulation of local
programs for homeless
persons and
the need for revision of such
regulations ;
e)
Eligibility standards for emergency housing; and
f)
Planning
for
the
development
of
permanent
housing
Opportunities for homeless persons.
While it shall review the entire system, the Commission shall
give
emphasis
to
assessing
the
problems
of
providing emergency
housing
to
families
and
to
responding
to
homeless
persons
now
residing on the streeis of New York.
§3.
The
Commission
shall
be
empowered
to
gather
information,
take evidence, and hold whatever hearings,
public or
private,
deemed
appropriate
by
the
Commission
to
ascertain
the
necessary facts.
§4.
Cooperation of City Agencies.
All departments and
agencies of the City are directed to furnish the Commission with such
facilities, services, and cooperation as it may request from time to
time.
§5.
This order shall take effect immediately.
