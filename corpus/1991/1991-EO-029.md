THE City oF NEw YorK
OFFICE OF THE Mayor
New Yoru, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 29
Octscber 1,
1992
OFFICE OF ENVIRONMENTAL COORDINATION
WHEREAS, section 192(e) of the Charter of the City of New
rork and recently promulgated rules of the City Planning Commission
provide
for
the
establishment
of
an
Office
of
Environmental
Coordination (OEC)
to
"provide assistance
to
all city agencies
in
fulfilling their environmental review responsibilities"; and
WHEREAS, the rules of the City Planning Commission assign
to
the
OEC
several
important
functions
in
order
to
assist
lead
agencies in the exercise of their environmental review responsibilities;
and
WHEREAS,
the
rules
of
the
City
Planning
Commission
providing for a more efficient system of environmental review in the
City of New York take effect on October 1, 1991;
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of
the City of New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section
1.
Establishment
of
Office
of
Environmental
Coordination.
There is hereby established within the Office of the
Mayor an Office of Environmental Coordination (OEC), to be headed
by a director who shall be appointed by and serve at the pleasure of

the Mayor.
The director shall report to and be directly accountable
to the Deputy Mayor for Planning and Development.
§2. Scope of Duties.
The OEC shall:
a.
Provide assistance
to city agencies
in fulfilling their
environmental review responsibilities.
b. Perform any environmental review function assigned to it
by a lead agency, except as provided in rules of the City Planning
Commission governing environmental review.
c. Assist City agencies in coordinating environmental review
when an action involves both City and state agencies, and when an
action or part thereof has been or will be reviewed by a federal
ugency.
d. Perform any other function required by rules of the City
Planning Commission governing environmental review.
$3.
Cooperation
and
Assistance.
The heads
of all
City
departments and agencies, including the Director of City Planning and
the
Commissioner
of
Environmental
Protection
pursuant
to
section
192(e) of the Charter, shall cooperate with and assist the OEC.
$4. This Order shall take effect immediately.
DAVID N. DINKINS
MAYOR
