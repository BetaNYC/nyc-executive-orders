THE City oF New YorRK
OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
New York, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 41
JULY 24 1992
THE MAYOR’S OFFICE FOR THE INCREASE THE PEACE VOLUNTEER CORPS
(Amendment of Executive Order No. 32, dated 12/16/91)
By the power vested in me as the Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered:
Section 1. The Mayor’s Office for the Increase the Peace Corps., established
by Executive Order No. 32, dated December 16, 1991, shall henceforth be known as the
Mayor’s Office for the Increase the Peace Volunteer Corps.
Section 2. The Increase the Peace Corps, established by Executive Order No.
32, dated December 16, 1991, shall henceforth be known as the Increase the Peace
Volunteer Corps.
Section 3.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
.
“
AVID N. DINKINS
MAYOR
