HIV HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES PLANNING COUNCIL
(Amendment of Executive Order No. 23, dated 1/18/91)
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered as follows:
Section 1.
Prior Order Amended. Section 3 of Executive Order No.
23, dated January 18, 1991, is hereby amended to read as follows:
Section 3.
Membership.
(a) The Planning
Council shall be appointed by the Mayor and
shall consist of not less than 35 and not more
than 45 members. The members of the Planning
Council shall serve at the pleasure of the Mayor
and from among them, the Mayor shall designate
a Chairperson.
(b)
The membership of the Planning Council
shall include representatives of health care
providers, community-based and AIDS service
organizations, social service providers, mental
health
care
providers,
local
public
health
agencies, hospital planning agencies or health
care planning agencies, affected communities
(including individuals with HIV/AIDS), nonelected community leaders, State government,
grantees of categorical grants for early

-2intervention services under subpart II of part C of
the Ryan White Act and the lead agency of any
Health Resources and Services Administration
adult
and
pediatric
HIV-related
care
demonstration project operating in the area to be
served.
At least one member of the Planning
Council shall represent separately each of these
specified categories.
(c)
Twenty-five (25) percent of the positions
on the Planning Council shall be allocated to
individuals with HIV/AIDS.
(d) the Mayor also shall appoint one member
that is mutually acceptable to the Mayor and the
County Executives of Westchester, Rockland and
Putnam Counties to represent these political
subdivisions or Health Resources and Services
Administration grant recipients therein on the
Planning Council.
(e¢) The Planning Council shall also include as
members the Commissioner of Mental Health,
Mental Retardation, and Alcoholism Services of
the City of New York, the Commissioner of
Health
of
the
City
of
New
York,
the
Administrator of the New York City Human
Resources Administration, the Director of the
Office of Management and Budget of the City of
New York, and the President of the Health and
Hospital Corporation, or their designees.
Such
designees
must
be
at
least
an
Assistant
Commissioner or the equivalent of the agency.
(f) The Mayor shall designate alternates for
members of the Planning Council who serve as
individuals with HIV/AIDS to serve in the
member's place if as a result of his or her illness
the member cannot perform the duties of a
Planning Council member.

(g) With respect to all members of the Planning
Council
other

enumerated

(h) There must be at least one member of the
Planning Council from each of New York City’s
boroughs.
(i) The following shall serve as non-voting, ex
officio representatives to the Planning Council:
Director of Mayor’s Office on Special Needs
Housing, Director of Mayor's Office on Drug

(j) In the event of a vacancy, the Planning
Council may within thirty (30) days after the
vacancy occurs recommend to the Mayor the
names of three individuals who would satisfy the
relevant qualifying criteria to fill that vacancy.
Section 2.
Effective Date. This Order shall take effect immediately.
