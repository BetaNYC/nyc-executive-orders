EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 38
JUNE 11, 1992
THE NEW YORK CITY PROCUREMENT TRAINING INSTITUTE
WHEREAS, the 1989 Charter amendments and the rules of the Procurement
Policy Board adopted pursuant thereto have established a new, uniform system for the
procurement of goods, services and construction by City agencies; and
WHEREAS, the Mayor, acting through the City Chief Procurement Officer,
is responsible for coordination and implementation of the City’s procurement function; and
WHEREAS, the training and professionalization of City agency procurement
personnel is essential to fully implement this new procurement system, to improve
purchasing decisions, and to produce savings to the City;
NOW “THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
—_ a. Creation of the Institute. There is hereby established a New
York City Procurement Training Institute.

Section 3. Training Institute Board
a.
The Institute shall be governed by a Board consisting of the Deputy
City Personnel Director for Personnel Development of the Department of Personnel, who
shall serve as Chair, the Director of the Mayor’s Office of Contracts, and a member of the
public having substantial expertise in procurement management to be appointed by the
Mayor for a two-year term upon the recommendation of the Procurement Policy Board.
Such public member shall serve without salary, except that he or she shall be entitled to
reimbursement for actual and necessary expenses incurred in the performance of official
duties as
a member of the Institute Board and shall also receive a per diem allowance when
rendering services-for the Institute Board at a rate established by the Mayor.

Section 4.
Agency Assistance. The Department of Personnel shall assist
the Institute in the administration of its training and education programs and shall award
and administer all contracts for instructors and other services necessary for the Institute to
conduct its activities. The Mayor’s Office of Contracts shall identify attendees for Institute
training and education programs, assess training needs for city personnel, and coordinate the
activities of the Institute with existing training and education programs conducted by City
agencies.
Section 5.
Effective Date. This Order shall take effect immediately.
