EXECUTIVE ORDER NO.
44
DECEMBER 3, 1992
DESIGNATION OF THE RESPONSIBLE AGENCY FOR FRANCHISES
TO BE AWARDED PURSUANT TO THE CITY CHARTER
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:
Section 1:
Pursuant to §§363 and 378 of the New York City Charter:
(a)
The Department of Transportation is designated the responsible
agency for the following types of franchises --
(1)
Bus lines,
(2)
Bus stop shelters,
(3)
Electric power distribution systems,
(4)
Tramways,
(5)
Below grade passenger railroad tacks and tunnels,
(6)
Self-cleaning automatic public toilets.
(b)
The Department of Business Services is designated
the
responsible agency for the following types of franchises --
(1)
~~ Railiroad freight and shipping lines,
(2)
Refrigeration distribution systems.
Section 2:
Pursuant to Chapter 48 of the New York City Charter,
the Department of Telecommunications and Energy shall be the responsible agency for
franchises relating to telecommunications as defined in that Chapter.
ection
3:
Executive Order No. 8 of 1990 is hereby repealed.
Section 4;
This Order shall take effect immediately.
