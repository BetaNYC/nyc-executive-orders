THe City oF NEw YORK
OFFICE OF THE Mayor
New Yorw, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 40
JULY 20, 1992
DEPUTY MAYORS
(Transfer of Responsibility for Supervision
of New York City Commission for the
United Nations and Consular Corps and International Business)
Amendment of Executive Order No.33
(December 27, 1991)
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:
Section 1. Subdivision c of section 4 of prior Executive Order No. 33,
dated December 27, 1991, is amended to read as follows:
c.
Supervise and coordinate the activities of the Departments of
Finance, Employment, Telecommunications and Energy, and Business Services; the Office
of Film, Theatre and Broadcasting; and the New York City Commission for the United
Nations and Consular Corps.
.
Section 2. Subdivision b of section 6 of such prior Executive Order is
amended to read as follows:
b. Act on behalf of the Mayor, either directly or through the Office of
City Legislative Affairs, the Office of State Legislative Affairs, or the Mayor’s Office in
Washington, D.C., with respect to all City, State and Federal legislative matters affecting the
City; direct the activities of the Mayor's legislative representatives in New York City,
Albany, and Washington, D.C.; represent the Mayor before City Council, Congressional and

-2-
State legislative committees; and maintain liaison with the Office of the President and all
Federal departments and agencies, the Office of the Governor and all State departments
and agencies, and the City Council.
Section 3.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
DAVID N. DINKIN
MAYOR
