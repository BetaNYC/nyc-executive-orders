EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 423
EXTENSION OF EMERGENCY ORDER REGARDING WAIVER OF CERTAIN LOCAL LAWS
RELATED TO DEMOLITION
October 8, 2013
WHEREAS, Executive Order 413, issued September 23, 2013, contains a proclamation
extending a state of emergency in the City of New York, and such proclamation remains in effect for a
period not to exceed thirty days or until rescinded, whichever occurs first; and
WHEREAS, on October 26, 2012, the Governor issued a Declaration of Emergency for
all counties in the State of New York, including the City of New York and contiguous counties, and on
July 15, 2013 extended such Declaration for certain counties including the City of New York, and such
declaration remains in effect for 90 days;
WHEREAS, Executive Order 234, issued February 5, 2013, contains an emergency
order regarding waiver of certain local laws related to demolition;
NOW, THEREFORE, it is hereby ordered:
§ 1.
The emergency order regarding waiver of certain local laws related to demolition
set forth in Executive Order 234 is hereby extended for five days.
§ 2.
This Order shall take effect immediately. It shall remain in effect for five (5)
days unless it is terminated or modified at an earlier date.
