EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 427
EXTENSION OF EMERGENCY ORDER TO SUSPEND ZONING PROVISIONS TO FACILITATE
RECONSTRUCTION IN ACCORDANCE WITH ENHANCED FLOOD RESISTANT CONSTRUCTION
STANDARDS
October 13, 2013
WHEREAS, Executive Order 413, issued September 23, 2013, contains a proclamation
extending a state of emergency in the City of New York, and such proclamation remains in effect for a period
not to exceed thirty days or until rescinded, whichever occurs first; and
WHEREAS, on October 26, 2012, the Governor issued a Declaration of Emergency for all
counties in the State of New York, including the City of New York and contiguous counties, and on July 15,
2013 extended such Declaration for certain counties including the City of New York, and such declaration
remains in effect for 90 days;
WHEREAS, Executive Order 233, issued February 5, 2013, contains an emergency order to
suspend zoning provisions to facilitate reconstruction in accordance with enhanced flood resistant construction
standards;
NOW, THEREFORE, it is hereby ordered:
§ 1.
The emergency order to suspend zoning provisions to facilitate reconstruction in
accordance with enhanced flood resistant construction standards set forth in Executive Order 233 is hereby
extended for five days.
§ 2.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
It shall remain in effect for five (5) days
unless it is terminated or modified at an earlier date.
