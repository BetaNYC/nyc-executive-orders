EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 463
December 27, 2013
AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER 306 REGARDING THE MAYOR’S OFFICE OF
DATA ANALYTICS
WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 306, dated April 17, 2013, established the Mayor’s
Office of Data Analytics: and
WHEREAS, it is essential for the operations of the Office of Data Analytics that the
Chief Analytics Officer continue to direct policies, collect data, initiate analyses and ensure
agency collaboration from within the Mayor’s Office, and therefore that the Chief Analytics
Officer continue to be an employee of the Office of the Mayor; and
WHEREAS, pursuant to a December 13, 2013 Memorandum of Understanding between
the Deputy Mayor for Operations, the Mayor’s Chief Advisor for Policy and Strategic and the
Commissioner of the
Department of Information
Technology
and
Telecommunications
(“DoITT”), other employees working in the Mayor’s Office of Data Analytics have been
transferred to DoITT but will continue to report to the Chief Analytics Officer; and
WHEREAS, DoITT reports to the Deputy Mayor for Operations, and
WHEREAS, pursuant to Executive Order No. 306, the Office of Data Analytics works
with agencies to implement data-driven solutions to City service delivery issues,
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Section 2 of Executive Order No. 306, dated, April 17, 2013, is amended to
read as follows:
§ 2.
The Office will be headed by a Chief Analytics Officer.
The Chief Analytics
Officer will report to the Deputy Mayor for Operations.
§ 2. This Order shall take effect immediately.
Michael R.,Womberg
Mayor
