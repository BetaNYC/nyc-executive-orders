THE

OFFICE

MAYOR
NEW YORK, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 459
December 20, 2013
AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER 175 REGARDING THE OFFICE OF HOUSING
RECOVERY OPERATIONS
WHEREAS, Executive Order 175, dated November 13, 2012, established the Mayor’s
Office of Housing Recovery Operations, and
WHEREAS, the City has made substantial progress in recovering from the impact of
Hurricane Sandy, but there is still much work to be done, and
WHEREAS, implementation of solutions to address the long-term housing needs of those
displaced by Hurricane Sandy has required enhanced engagement with the agencies under the
purview of the Deputy Mayor for Operations;
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:
Section 1. Section 2 of Executive Order 175, dated, November 13, 2012, is amended to
read as follows:
§ 2.
The Office will be headed by a Director of Housing Recovery Operations.
The
Director will report to the Deputy Mayor for Operations.
§ 2. This Order shall take effect immediately.
Michael R’Bloomberg
Mayor
