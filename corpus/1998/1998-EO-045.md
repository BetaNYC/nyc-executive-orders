ESTABLISHMENT OF THE OFFICE FOR THE MILLENNIUM
WHEREAS, people throughout the world anticipate celebration of the new
millennium with the hope and expectation that it will usher in an era of peace and prosperity;
and
WHEREAS, the stature of New York City as the undisputed financial and cultural
capital of the world, attracting visitors and tourists from across the nation and around the world,
makes it particularly appropriate for the City to host cultural activities and events in celebration
of the millennium; and
WHEREAS, it is necessary and appropriate that the City government establish
an office within the office of the Mayor to lead the effort to plan and coordinate the activities
and events that will take place in the City in celebration of the millennium,
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:

Establishment of the Office for the Millennium. (1) There is hereby
Section 1.
established within the Office of the Mayor, the Office for the Millennium.
(2) The Mayor shall appoint a City employee to be the executive director of the
Office for the Millennium and such other staff as in his judgment shall be necessary to carry
out the functions of such office.
§ 2. Establishment of Committee for NYC 2000. There shall be a committee
