EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 113

DESIGNATION OF DEPARTMENT OF SANITATION TO ENFORCE
 GENERAL BUSINESS LAW SECTION 397-a

February 20, 2008

WHEREAS, Chapter 3 of the Laws of 2008, which amends section 397-a of the General
Business Law in relation to distribution of unsolicited advertising on private property, took effect
on January 28, 2008; and
WHEREAS, subdivision 4 of section 397-a of the General Business Law, as amended,
provides for the provisions of such section to be enforced by “an agency or agencies, as
designated by the mayor” of a city with a population of one million or more;

NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:

Section 1. The Department of Sanitation is designated to enforce the provisions of
section 397-a of the General Business Law.

§ 2.
This Order shall take effect immediately.

________________________

Michael R. Bloomberg

Mayor
