EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 1

JANUARY 1, 2002

CONTINUATION OF PRIOR EXECUTIVE ORDERS

By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
Is ordered:
Section 1. All Executive Orders in effect on December 31, 2001 are hereby
continued unless specifically revoked, revised or superseded by a subsequently issued
Executive Order.
§ 2.
This Order shall take effect immediately.

______________________
Michael R. Bloomberg
Mayor
