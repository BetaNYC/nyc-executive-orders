EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 10

MARCH 21, 2002

DELEGATION OF CERTAIN FUNCTIONS, POWERS AND DUTIES TO DIRECTOR
OF MANAGEMENT AND BUDGET

By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered:

Section 1. The Director of Management and Budget shall act for and on behalf of
the Mayor and at the direction of the Mayor in the exercise of all functions, powers and
duties which the Mayor may have pursuant to the New York State Local Finance Law, as
the same may from time to time be amended, the New York State Financial Emergency
Act for the City of New York, as the same may from time to time be amended, Article 10
of the Public Authorities Law, as the same may from time to time be amended, the New
York City Municipal Water Finance Authority Act, as the same may from time to time be
amended (with the exception of the Mayoral power to appoint directors pursuant to
Section 1045-c and Section 1045-f of such act), and the New York City Transitional
Finance Authority Act, as the same may from time to time be amended, including, but not
limited to, full power to execute for and on behalf of the Mayor, any agreement,
amendment to such agreement or other document pursuant to such laws. The exercise of
functions, powers and duties for, on behalf of, and at the direction of the Mayor pursuant
to this delegation shall not require the consent in writing of the Mayor or any other
official of the City.
Section 2. Executive Order No. 74, dated April 30, 1984, is hereby REVOKED.
Section 3. This Order shall take effect immediately.

______________________
Michael R. Bloomberg
Mayor
