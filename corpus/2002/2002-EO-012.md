EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 12

MAY 13, 2002

AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 6 DATED FEBRUARY 8, 2002
RELATING TO THE MAYOR'S COMMITTEE ON APPOINTMENTS

By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered:
Section 1. Subdivisions a and h of Section 2 of Executive Order No. 6, dated
February 8, 2002, are amended to read as follows:
(a)
The Committee shall be appointed by the Mayor and shall.consist of no
fewer than five or more than ten members, including a Chair. The Committee Chair may,
in his or her discretion, designate adjunct members who shall function under section three
as voting members with regard to appointments and reappointments to specific agencies,
commissions, boards or other public bodies. Adjunct members, if so designated, shall
have such expertise or knowledge as will assist the Committee in making a reasoned,
informed and impartial evaluation of candidates for appointments to agencies, boards,
commissions and other public bodies.
(b)
Members shall serve at the pleasure of the Mayor. The Mayor shall
designate the Committee Chair. Adjunct members shall function under section three as
voting members until the appointments or reappointments for which they have been
designated become final, and such adjunct members may be redesignated at the discretion
of the Committee Chair.
§2.
This Order shall take effect immediately.

______________________
Michael R. Bloomberg
Mayor
