EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 4

JANUARY 9, 2002

AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 2 DATED JANUARY 1, 2002
RELATING TO DEPUTY MAYORS

By the power vested in me as Mayor, of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered:
Section 1. Subdivision (d) of Section 4 of Executive Order No. 2, dated January 1,
2002, is revoked.
§2.
This Order shall take effect immediately.

Michael R. Bloomberg
Mayor
