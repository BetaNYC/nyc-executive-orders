EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 27

AUGUST 7, 2002

REVOCATION OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 26

By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered:
Section 1. Executive Order No. 26, dated August 6, 2002, is hereby revoked.
§ 2. This executive order shall take effect immediately.

______________________
Michael R. Bloomberg
Mayor
