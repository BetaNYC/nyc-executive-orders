EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 53
November 21, 2001
COMMISSIONER FOR THE UNITED NATIONS,
THE CONSULAR CORPS AND PROTOCOL
BY THE POWER vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:
Section 1. Section 2 of Executive Order No. 35 dated July 10, 1997 is
hereby amended to read as follows:
The Commission shall be headed by a Commissioner for the United
Nations, the Consular Corps and Protocol. The Commissioner shall serve without salary
unless the Mayor determines otherwise. The Commissioner shall serve at the pleasure of
the Mayor and be the Mayor’s personal representative.
Section 2. This Order shall take effect immediately.
Rudolph W. Giuliani
Mayor
