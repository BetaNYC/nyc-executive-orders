WORKER'S COMPENSATION COVERAGE FOR CITY EMPLOYEES
NOT COVERED TO DATE,
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York it is hereby
ordered:
Section 1.
Pursuant to the Worker's Compensation Law, Article 1,
Section 3, Subdivision 1, Group 19 ~ which permits any municipal corporation to
bring its employees or officers, eleetive or appointed otherwise, within the
Worker's Compensation Law by appropriate action of the legislative or
governmental body of the municipal corporation - all persons employed by the
City in any agency except persons employed by any publie benefit corporation or
the Board of Edueation and uniformed employees of the Police, Fire and
Sanitation Departments, who are not otherwise covered under the Workers!
Compensation Law and who, as of this date, have not been brought within the
coverage of such law by appropriate action of the Mayor, the City Council or
the Board of Estimate are hereby brought within the coverage of the Workers!
Compensation Law.
Section 2.
This Executive Order shall take effect immediately.
