DEPARTMENT OF EMPLOYMENT
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York it is
hereby ordered as follows:
Section 1.
Continuation of Department; Commissioner.
There is continued
in the Executive Office of the Mayor a Department of Employment
("Department").
The head of the Department shall be Commissioner of
Employment, who shall be appointed by the Mayor and who shali serve at the
Mayor's pleasure.
Section 2.
Deputy.
The Commissioner may appoint and at pleasure
remove a deputy commissioner.
Section 3.
Powers and duties.
The Department shall have the following
power and duties:
a.
to administer employment and training programs for
unemployed, underemployed and economically
disadvantaged youth and adults;
b.
to disburse available city, state and federal funds to
employment and training programs throughout the city
and, when practical, coordinate such funds with
available funds from the private sector;
ce.
to advise and assist the Mayor in developing policies
designed to meet the needs of the unemployed and
economically disadvantaged;
d.
to make such studies and reports regarding needs of the
unemployed and economically disadvantaged as the
Commissioner may deem appropriate or as may be
requested by the Mayor;
e,
to maintain, operate and control such programs and
facilities as may be necessary or required for the
proper administration of the Department.
Section 4.
Effective Date.
This order shall take effect immediately.
