DEFERRED COMPENSATION BOARD
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
ordered:
Section 1.
Prior Order Amended.
Section 1 of prior Executive Order
No. 81, dated April 16, 1985 is hereby amended to read as follows:
"Section 1.
Deferred Compensation Board
Established.
The Deferred Compensation Board
(hereinafter called the Board) is hereby
established in the Office of the Mayor to take
all steps necessary to the preparation and
implementation of deferred compensation plans
pursuant to Section 401(k) and/or Section 457
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1954, as
amended, and any relevant provisions of State
law for the following persons:
(a) employees of the City of New York in
titles not certified to or represented by a
collective bargaining representative pursuant to
Article 14 of the Civil Service Law (the Taylor
Law), and Chapter 54 of the Administrative
Code of the City of New York (the New York
City Collective Bargaining Law).
Employees of
the City of New York who are included in a
unit of employees covered by a collective
bargaining agreement may participate in the
plan if the colleetive bargaining agreement
covering such employees provides for coverage
under the plan,

-Q-
(b) employees of a public corporation or public
authority operating in whole within the City of
New York other than employees who are
included in a unit of employees covered by a
colieective bargaining agreement, provided the
governing body of such public corporation or
public authority adopts the plan by appropriate
resolution or other legal action and such
participation is approved by the Board.
Employees of such public corporation or public
authority who are ineluded in a unit of
empioyees covered by a collective bargaining
agreement may participate in the plan if the
collective bargaining agreement covering such
employees provides for coverage under the
plan.
Section 2.
Effective Date.
This Executive Order shall take effect
immediately.
