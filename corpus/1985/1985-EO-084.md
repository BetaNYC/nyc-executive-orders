THE City oF NEw York
OFFICE OF THE Mayor
New York, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 84
June 17, 1985
Amendment of Executive Order No. 83
Dated July 26, 1985
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered;
Section 1.
Prior Order Amended.
Section 5 of Executive Order No.
83, dated July 26, 1973 is hereby amended to add subsection g, which
shall provide as follows:
gs.
For all grievances as defined in Section 5b(C):
(A) the
grievance must be brought in writing; and (B) no monetary
award shall in any event cover any period prior to the date
of the filing of the Step I grievance unless such grievance
has been filed within thirty (30) days of the assignment to
alleged out-of-title work."
Section 2.
Effective Date.
This order shall take effect immediately.
ON Val
Edward I. Koch
MAYOR
