THE CITY OF NEW YORK
OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
NEW YORK, N. Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER No. I
JANUARY I, 2026
PRIOR EXECUTIVE ORDERS
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section I. All Executive Orders issued prior to September 26, 2024, and in effect on
December 3 I, 2025, are hereby continued unless specifically revoked, revised, or superseded by a
subsequently issued Executive Order. All Executive Orders issued on or after September 26, 2024,
and in effect on December 3 I, 2025, are hereby revoked.
§ 2. This Order shall not be construed to affect the provisions of Emergency Executive
Orders issued pursuant to section 24 of the Executive Law, where such provisions are in effect as
of January I, 2026.
§ 3. This Order shall take effect immediately.
Zohran Kwame Mamdani
Mayor
