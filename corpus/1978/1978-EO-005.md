EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 5
JANUARY 24, 1978
ESTABLISHMENT OF A SOUTH BRONX COORDINATING COUNCIL
foll By the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York, it is hereby ordered as
‘ollows :
Section 1.
A South Bronx Coordinating Council is hereby created, to be headed by the
Mayor. The Council shall consist of the Mayor, the Deputy Mayor for Economic Development,
the Deputy Mayor for Management, the Chairman of the City
Planning Commission, the Cammissioner of the Department of Housing Preservation and Development, the Director of Management and Budget and the Corporation Counsel.
§2. The Mayor's Coordinator for South Bronx Redevelopment shall serve as Executive
Director of the Council.
:
§3.
The Council shafl coordinate planning, development and related efforts in the South
Bronx for the City.
§4. All appropriate City agencies, including without limitation the Department of Employment, the Department of Buildings, the Human Resources Administration, the Department
of
Transportation, the Department of Parks and Recreation, the Department of Health, the
Department of Environmental Protection and the Department of General Services, are directed
to assign senior personnel to assist the Council in its efforts, to attend meetings requested by the
Council and to work with the Council members to carry out the national, State and City policy
objectives and operations in the South Bronx.
§5.
This
Order shall take effect immediately.
