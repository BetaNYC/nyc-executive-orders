OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 19
SEPTEMBER 7, 1978
enn
REAL PROPERTY TAX ASSESSMENTS
By the power vested in me as Mayor of The City af New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1. Evaluation of Real Property--With a view to satisfying the requirements of the
Real Property Tax Law as amended by the Laws of 1978, chapter 476, the Finance Department,
the Tax Commission, and the Real Property Assessment Department, insofar as each has
jurisdiction over real property assessments in the City, shall Gile all necessary applications with
the appropriate State agencies and take any and all steps necessary to move toward physical
revaluation of all real property within the boundaries of the City at 100 per cent of full value.
.
Effective Date—This order shall take effect immediately and shall be implemented
with all deliberate speed.
EDWARD I. KOCH, 'Mayor.
