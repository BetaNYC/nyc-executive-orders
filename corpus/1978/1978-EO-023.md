EXECUTIVE ORDER NO, 23
i
October 13, 1978
EMERGENCY MEDICAL SERVICE
BY VIRTUE OF THE POWER VESTED IN ME AS THE MAYOR OF THE CITY
of New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Prior Executive Order. Section 2(a) of Executive Order No. 15, dated July 17,
1978, is hereby amended to read as follows :
(a) The Council shall be composed of five members appointed by the Mayor. The special
advisor to the Mayor for Health Affairs shall be a member and shail serve as chairman of the
Council. Two members shalt be health care professionals with substantial experience in emergency care ; and two members shall be representatives of the general public and shall not be
roviders of health care,
2.
Affective Date. This Order shall take effect immediately
EDWARD I. KOCH, Mayor.
