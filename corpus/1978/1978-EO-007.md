OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 7
MARCH 6, 1978
BY THE POWER VESTED IN ME AS MAYOR OF THE CITY OF NEW YORK,
it is hereby ordered :
Section 1.
The Model Cities Administration in the Office of the Mayor is hereby renamed
and shall hereinafter be known as the Mayor's Office of Model Cities.
.
The position of Model Cities Administrator shall hereinafter be known as the Director
of Model Cities.
i The Director shall be appointed by the Mayor and serve at his pleasure,
4.
This Order shall take effect taamediately.
ml15-21
EDWARD I. KOCH, Mayor.
