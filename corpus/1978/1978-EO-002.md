EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 2
JANUARY 1, 1978
DEPUTY MAYORS
BY THE POWER VESTED IN ME AS MAYOR OF THE CITY OF NEW YORK,
it is hereby ordered :
Section 1.
There shall be seven Deputy Mayors. One of them shall be designated Deputy
Mayor for Management, one shali be designated Deputy Mayor for Intergovernmental Relations,
one shall be designated Deputy Mayor for Personnel and Labor Relations, one shall be designated
Deputy Mayor
for Policy, one shall be designated Deputy Mayor for Economic Development,
one shall be designated Deputy Mayor for Finance, and one shall be designated Deputy Mayor
for Criminal Justice.
§2.
The Deputy Mayor for Management shall:
(a) Supervise and coordinate the operations of all administrations, departments, boards
and agencies, except as otherwise provided in this Order or as the Mayor may direct.
(b) Maintain liaison with and review the activities of the Board of Education and the
Board of Higher Education of The City of
New York.
(c) Represent the Mayor when so directed on any board, committee, commission or
corporation of which the Mayor is a member.
Perform such other duties as the Mayor may direct.
§3.
The Deputy Mayor for Intergovernmental Relations shall:
(a) In the absence of the Mayor from sessions of the Board of Estimate act on behalf
of the Mayor at such sessions of the Board of Estimate.
(b) Act a behalf of the Mayor with respect to all Federal, State and local legislative
matters affectihg the City; direct the activities of the Mayor's legislative representative,
the Albany office, and the Washington, D.C. office; represent the
Mayor before Congressional and State legislative committees, maintain liaison with the Office of the President .
and ali Federal departments and agencies thereof, the Office of the Governor and all State
departments and agencies thereof, the Board of Estimate and the City Council; and supervise the activities of the New York City Commission for the United Nations and for the
Consular Corps.
(c) Serve as the Mayor’s representative on and maintain liaison with regional bodies
and intergovernmental agencies operating within the New York metropolitan region.
(d) Maintain liaison with the United
States Conference of Mayors, the National League
of Cities, the New York State Conference of Mayors and other similar bodies.
(e) Represent the Mayor when so directed by the Mayor on any board, committee,
commission or corporation of which the Mayor is a member.
({) Perform such other duties as the Mayor may direct.
§4.
The Deputy Mayor for Personnel and Labor Relations shall:
(a) Supervise and coordinate the operations of the Department of Personnel and the
Office of Municipal Labor Relations; conduct all labor negotiations to which the City is a
party; represent the (Mayor and act on his behalf in connection with all labor negotiations
in which the City has an interest, including, but not limited to, negotiations conducted by
or on behalf of all public benefit corporations located within the City, the Board of Education and the Board of Higher Education of The City of New York.
(b) Represent the Mayor when so directed on any board, committee, commission or
corporation of which the {Mayor is a member.
(c) Perform such other duties as the Mayor may direct.
§5.
The Deputy Mayor for Policy shall:
(a) Advise and assist the Mayor on matters of City policy.
(b) Maintain liaison with and review the activities of the Health and Hospitals Corporation of The City of New York.
(c) Represent the Mayor when so directed by the Mayor on any board, committee,
commission or corporation of which the Mayor is a member.
(d) Perform the duties specifically referred to in Section 7 of.the New York City
Charter and other duties as the Mayor may direct.
§6.
The Deputy Mayor for Economic Development shall :
(a) Head the economic development program, supervise and coordinate the efforts to
foster the economic growth of the City.
(b) Direct the operations of the Office for Economic Development.
(c) Represent the Mayor when so directed by the Mayor on any board, committee,
commission or corporation of which the Mayor is a member.
(d) Perform such other duties as the
Mayor may direct,
§7.
The Deputy Mayor for Finance shall ;
(a) Supervise and coordinate the City's fiscal operations, including the operations of
the Office of Management and Budget.
(b) Act on behalf of and at the direction of the Mayor in exercising

of the Act between The City of New York and the Municipal Assistance Corporation for
The City of New York, any certificate pursuant to Section 3037 of the Act, and any other
document in connection with the Act. The exercise of functions, powers and duties for,
on behalf of and at the direction of the Mayor by such Deputy Mayor pursuant to this delegation shall not require the consent in writing of the Mayor or any other official of the City.
(d) For the purposes of Article 2, Section 9 of the Public Officers Law, notwithstanding
