OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 22
September 14, 1978
EFFECTIVENESS OF LOCAL LAW NO. 438 OF 1978
BY VIRTUE OF THE POWER VESTED IN ME AS THE MAYOR OF THE CITY
of New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1. Local Law No. 438 of June 20, 1978, a local law to amend the administrative
code of the city of New York to provide for the designation of a street area in lower Manhattan as “Louise Nevelson Plaza,” shall take effect immediately.

EDWARD I. KOCH, Mayor.
