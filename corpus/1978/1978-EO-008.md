EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 8
MARCH 8, 1978
BY THE POWER VESTED IN ME AS MAYOR OF THE CITY OF NEW YORK,
it is hereby ordered that:
Section 1.
Section 1 of Executive Order No. 3 of January 12, 1978, is hereby revoked, and
the Special Advisor to the Mayor on Health Services, Henri Axel Schupf, shall succeed to the |
powers and duties of the Health Services Administrator as specified in Chapter 1016 of the Laws
of 1969 (Section 7381 et seq. of the Unconsolidated Laws of New York).
:
§2. This Order shall take effect immediately,
mi5-21
EDWARD IL. KOCH, Mayor.
