THE CITY OF NEW YORK
OFFICE OF THE
MAYOR
NEW YORK, N. Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 60
August 31, 2020
DESIGNATION OF THE DEPARTMENT OF FINANCE AS THE ADMINISTERING
AGENCY FOR A SUSTAINABLE ENERGY LOAN PROGRAM PURSUANT
TO CHAPTER 30 OF TITLE 11 OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE CODE
WHEREAS, effective May 19, 2019, Local Law No. 96 for the year 2019 added a new
chapter 30 to title 11 of the Administrative Code, in relation to establishing a sustainable energy
loan program in the City of New York; and
WHEREAS, section 11-3008 of the Administrative Code authorizes the Mayor to
designate one or more offices or agencies to implement, administer and enforce the provisions of
such chapter;
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:
Section 1. The Department of Finance is designated as the administering agency for the
sustainable energy loan program, provided, however that the Office of Long-Term Planning and
Sustainability shall provide such assistance as is necessary for the implementation and
administration of such program, including with regard to the development and amendment of
program standards and guidelines, and the determination of eligibility for lenders, borrowers,
energy audit providers, and renewable energy system feasibility study providers participating in
such program.
§ 2. This Order shall take effect immediately.
Bill de Blasio,
MAYOR
