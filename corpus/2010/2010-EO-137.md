EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 137

July 15, 2010

AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 121, RELATING TO MAYOR’S OFFICE OF
CONTRACT SERVICES

WHEREAS, Executive Order number 121, dated August 11, 2008, sets forth the
functions and duties of the Office of Contract Services; and

WHEREAS, § 14(2) of the Urban Development Corporation Act allows the City to
cooperate with The New York State Urban Development Corporation d/b/a The Empire State
Development Corporation by selling, leasing, granting or conveying an interest in certain
properties of which the City is the fee owner when deemed necessary or convenient for the City;

NOW THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:

Section 1. In addition to performing the functions and duties specified in Executive
Order No. 121, the Director of the Office of Contract Services is authorized to execute all
authorizations, approvals, certifications and resolutions to be made or given by the Mayor
pursuant to § 14(2) of the Urban Development Corporation Act.

§ 2. In addition to holding the public hearings specified in Executive Order No. 121, the
Director, and staff of the Office of Contract Services designated by the Director, are authorized
to coordinate and, where applicable, hold any public hearings or other proceedings, including
required public notice thereof, to be held pursuant to section § 14(2) of the Urban Development
Corporation Act.

§ 3. This order shall take effect immediately.
