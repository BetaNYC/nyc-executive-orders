AMENDMENT TO EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 55
DATED MARCH 29, 1976
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York (the "City"), pursuant to the City Charter and the
Local Finance Law and in ‘the exercise of the powers of the
Finance Board of the City, it is ordered:
Section 1.
Amendment of Executive Order No. 55.
Executive Order No. 55, dated March 29, 1976, entitled,
"Delegation of Powers of the Finance Board", as amended by
Executive Order No. 62, dated July 19, 1976, Executive Order
No. 68, dated August 1, 1983 and Executive Order No. 97,
dated July 24, 1986 ("Executive Order No. 55") is hereby
amended by changing Subdivision 3 of Paragraph B of
Section 2 of Executive Order No. 55 to read as follows:
(3)
Obligations issued in registered or bearer
form shall be authenticated by the manual
countersignature of the Fiscal Agent of the
City ‘or by the City Clerk or a Deputy City
Clerk.
Section 2.
Effective Date.
This order shall take
effect immediately.
Edward
I”.
