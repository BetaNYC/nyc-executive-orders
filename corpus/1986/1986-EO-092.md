MAYOR'S COMMITTEE ON APPOINTMENTS
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Prior Order Amended.
Section 4(a) of prior
Executive Order No. 90, dated March 13, 1986, is hereby
amended to read as follows:
"Section 4.
Committee Membership.
(a)
The Committee shall consist of five
members including a chair, each residing or
having a principal place of business in the City
of New York, appointed by the Mayor.
Such
members may, in their discretion, designate
adjunct members who shall function under section
two as voting members with regard to appointments
and reappointments to specific commissions,
boards or other public bodies.
Adjunct members,
if so designated, will be professionals or
knowledgeable persons of high standing with
expertise appropriate for enabling the Committee
to make a reasoned, informed and impartial
evaluation of candidates for the offices referred
to in section one.
If so designated, there shall
be between two and four adjunct members for each
commission, board or other public body for which
candidates are evaluated, according to the
determination of the members."

Section 2.
Effective Date.
This Executive Order shall
take effect immediately.
Edward I. Koch
MAYOR
