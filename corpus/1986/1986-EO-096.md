OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
New York, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 96
July 14,
1986
HEALTH SERVICES ADMINISTRATOR
By virtue of the power vested in me as Mayor of the
City of New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Sara L. Kellerman is hereby appointed as
the Special Advisor to the Mayor for Health Services,
without compensation and as such she succeeds to the powers
and duties of the Health Services Administrator as specified
in Chapter 1016 of the Law of 1969 (Section 7381 et. seg. of
the Unconsolidated Laws of New York).
Section 2.
Prior Order Revoked.
Section 1 of
Executive Order No. 88, dated January 10, 1986, is hereby
revoked.
Section 3.
Effective Date.
This Order shall be
effective immediately.
