THE City oF New York
OFFICE OF THE Mayvor
New York, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 97
July 24, 1986
AMENDMENT TO EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 55
DATED MARCH 29, 1976
By the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New
York (the "City"),
pursuant
to the
City Charter and the
Local
Finance Law and in the exercise of the powers of the Finance Board
of the City, it is hereby ordered:
Section
1.
Amendment
of
Executive
Order
No.
45.
Executive Order No. 55, dated March 29, 1976, entitled "Delegation of
Powers of the Finance Board", as amended by Executive Order No.
62, dated July 19, 1976 and Executive Order No. 68, dated August 1,
1983 ("Executive Order No. 55"), is hereby amended by:
A.
Changing Subdivision 1 of Paragraph B of Section 2 of
Executive Order No. 55 to read as follows:
(1)
The seal of the City shall be impressed upon or
the facsimile seal of the City imprinted upon each
Obligation, and each Obligation shall be attested
by the manual or facsimile signature of the City
Clerk or a Deputy City Clerk.
B.
Changing Subdivision 3 of Paragraph B of Section 2 of
Executive Order No. 55 to read as follows:
(3)
Obligations issued in registered or bearer form
shall
be
authenticated
by
the
manual
countersignature of the Fiscal Agent of the City,
except
that
any
or
all
Obligations
issued
in
bearer
form
may
be
authenticated
in
the
alternative by the manual signature of the City
Clerk or a Deputy City Clerk.
Section 2.
Effective Date.
This Order shall take effect
immediately.
