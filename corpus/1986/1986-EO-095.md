THE City oF New YorRK
OFFICE OF THE MaYoR
New York, N.Y. 10007
Executive Order No. 95
July 8, 1986
NEW YORK CITY ENERGY
AND TELECOMMUNICATIONS OFFICE
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:
Section 1;
Office Established.
The New York City Energy and
Telecommunications Office (the "Office") is hereby established in the Office
of the Mayor.
The Office shall be headed by a Director, who shall also
serve as the Director of the New York City Publie Utility Service
(NYCPUS"),
The Director shall report to the Deputy Mayor for Finance and
Economic Development.
Section 2.
Responsibilities.
The Office shall (a) have overall
responsibility for the development and coordination of energy policy and
telecommunications policy for the City, and (b) exercise all of the functions,
powers and duties of the public utility service established by Chapter 73 of
the New York City Administrative Code.
Section 3.
Staff and Expenses.
The Office shall have such staff and
incur such expenses necessary for it to perform its responsibilities and
effectuate its purposes.
Section 4.
Annual Report.
The Office shall report annually to the
Mayor on its activities and shall prepare such other reports as the Mayor
shall request.
Section 5.
Effective Date.
This Order shall take effect immediately
and supersedes Executive Order No. 67, dated January 24, 1983, which is
hereby revoked.
Edward I. Koch
MAYOR
