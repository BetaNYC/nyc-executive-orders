THE City oF New York
OFFICE OF THE Mayor
New YorK, N.Y. 10007
Executive Order No. 100
September 5, 1986
OFFICE OF COMPUTER PLANS AND CONTROLS
IN THE OFFICE OF THE DIRECTOR OF OPERATIONS
WHEREAS, it is necessary to coordinate and provide for the development of
computer systems which will improve agency operations, service levels and
management information; and it is necessary to insure the timely and competitive
acquisition of data processing goods and services; and it is necessary to
coordinate systems which have common applicability to more than one agency or
which require data collected or managed by another agency or more than one
agency; and it is necessary that the City develop and maintain a coordinated
strategy for the effective and efficient use of information systems and to insure
that agency plans are consistent with this strategy.
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered:
,
Section 1.
Office Established.
There is hereby created in the Office of
the Mayor an Office of Computer Plans and Controls to be headed by the
Director of Operations which will insure the elimination of duplicative effort, the
competitive acquisition and efficient use of computer resources and the
development of state of the art systems responsive to the requirements of the
City.
It shall have the following functions and duties:
1.
To develop and maintain a long-range computer system
strategy for the City of New York.
2.
To review and approve agency information system plans.
3.
To develop an annual capital budget request for computer
equipment; and to review and approve the expenditure of.
capital funds for computer equipment.
