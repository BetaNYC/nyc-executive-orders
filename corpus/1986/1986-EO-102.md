OFFICE OF THE AUDITOR GENERAL
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Office Established,
The Office of the
Auditor General (the “Office") is hereby established in the
Office of the Mayor.
The Office Shall be headed by the
Auditor General, who shall be appointed by, report to and
Serve at the pleasure of the Mayor.
