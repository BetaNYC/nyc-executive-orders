THE City oF NEw York
OFFICE OF THE Mayor
New York, N.Y. 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 88
January 10, 1986
HEALTH SERVICES ADMINISTRATOR
By virtue of the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it
‘is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Victor E. Botnick shall succeed to the powers and duties of the
Health Services Administrator as specified in Chapter 1016 of the Laws of 1969
(Section 7381 et. seq. of the Unconsolidated Laws of New York).
Section 2.
Prior Orders Revoked.
Executive Orders No. 71, dated March 8,
1984, and No. 79, dated October 5, 1984, are hereby revoked.
Seetion 3.
Effective Date.
This Order shall be effective immediately.
Fre Cue
Edward I. Koch
MAYOR
