DESIGNATION OF FIRE DEPARTMENT AS THE AGENCY RESPONSIBLE FOR THE
FILING OF CERTIFICATES OF EXPENSES RELATING TO COSTS INCURRED BY
THE DEPARTMENT IN RESPONDING TO EMERGENCIES INVOLVING THE
RELEASE OR THREAT OF RELEASE OF HAZARDOUS SUBSTANCES INTO THE
ENVIRONMENT
By the power vested in me as the Mayor of the City of New York, it hereby is
ordered:
Section 1. Pursuant to § 24-605 of the Administrative Code of the Citv of New-
York, the Fire Department is designated the agency authorized to receive and file with the City
Collector certificates of expenses for Fire Department costs incurred in responding to
emergencies involving a release or substantial threat of a release of a hazardous substance into
the environment.
Such certificates shall be prepared in accordance with § 24-606 of the
Administrative Code, shall set forth the work done and the expenses incurred, shall certify that
such expenses were necessary and proper in the exercise of the Department’s lawful powers and
shall, where applicable, apportion such expenses among lots or buildings belonging to different
