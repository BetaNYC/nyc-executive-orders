OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 95
DECEMBER 2, 1977
DESIGNATION OF THE COMMISSIONER OF HEALTH TO SUCCEED TO THE
POWERS AND RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE HEALTH SERVICES
ADMINISTRATOR
Whereas, On [May 6, 1977, Local Law 25 was signed resulting in the abolition of the
Health Services Administration and the establishment of an independent Department of Health
and Department of Mental Health, Mental Retardation and Alcoholism
Services: and
Whereas, Certain powers and duties are vested in the Health Services Administrator by
Chapter 1016 of the Laws of 1969.
Now, therefore, by virtue of the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York, it
is hereby ordered that :
Section 1.
The Commissioner of Health shall succeed to the powers and duties of the
Health Services Administrator as specified in Chapter 1016 of the Laws of 1969.
§2.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
d16-22
ABRAHAM D. BEAME, Mayor.
