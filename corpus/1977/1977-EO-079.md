AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 76 DATED FEBRUARY 10, 1977
February 10, 1977, which establishes a Westway Expediting Council.
is hereby ordered as follows:
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 79
FEBRUARY 23, 1977
Whereas, It is necessary and desirable to amend Executive Order No. 76, dated
Now, therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York, it
Section 1.
Sections 1, 2 and 3 of Executive Order No. 76, dated February 10, 1977,
are hereby amended to read as follows:
“Section 1.
A Westway Expediting Council is hereby created, consisting of the
First Deputy Mayor, who shall be Chairman, the Transportation Administrator, the
Director of Construction, the Chairman of the City Planning Commission, the Carporation Counsel and the Director of the Office of Management and Budget.
§2.
The Transportation Administrator will coordinate all technical activities
relating to the expeditious preparation of the official roadway map and the Planning
Commission Chairman will coordinate land use and planning activities.
The Director of Construction will be responsible for coordinating the expeditious
relocation and development of public facilities and continue as liaison to the construction industry.
The Corporation Counsel and the Director of the Office of Management and Budget
will provide appropriate support services.
§
All agencies are directed to assign top policy level pcrsonnel to assist in this
effort when requested by the Council. In particular, the Commissioner of Sanitation.
the Administrator of Environmental Protection, the Commissioner of Water Resources
and the Commissioner of Ports and Terminals are requested to attend Council meetings
as required, and work directly with the members to carry out the goals and objectives
of building Westway under the overall direction of the First Deputy Mayor or his
designated representative.”
§2.
This Order shall take effect immediately,

ABRAHAM D. BEAME, Mayor.
