OFFICE OF THE MAYOR
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO, 83
APRIL. 20, 1977
MAYOR'S EMERGENCY CONTROL BOARD
Whereas, The Mayor’s Emergency Control Board was established to coordinate the
City’s response to natural or accidental crises, with the Mayor as Chairman, First Deputy
Mayor as Deputy Chairman, and consisting of Administrators of Municipal Services Administration, Transportation Administration, Environmental Protection Administration:
Commissioners of Fire, Health, Police, Public Works, Traffic, Sanitation, Water Resources,
Social Services; President, Health and Hospitals Corporation; Chairman, Metropolitan
Transit Authority and other agency heads on an as-needed basis; and
Whereas, The Board is further charged with preparations of plans designed to contain
and control emergency conditions caused by a natural or accidental disaster, maintain
essential services for the duration and restore full municipal services in a post-disaster
environment ; and
Whereas, The Police Commissioner, as the local Director of Civil Defense, is fully
familiar with the steps
and procedures needed to respond in an emergency situation; and
Whereas, It is vital to establish direct coordination between the
Police Commissioner
and the Emergency Controf Board to assure proper functioning of that Board and maximum
effectiveness to meet emergency situations.
Now, therefore, by the powers vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered :
Section 1.
The Police Commissioner is hereby appginted Director of the Mayor’s
Emergency Control Board as established by Executive
Order No. 107, dated December 22,
1961,
for which he shall receive no salary or additional compensation.
§2.
The Police Commissioner shall continue to function as the Director of Civil
Preparedness and as the local Director of Civil Defense, pursuant to Executive Order No.
53, dated March 5, 1976, with the powers of a local Director of Civil Defense under the
Laws of 1951, Chapter 784, as amended.
§3. The Emergency Control Board shall meet when convened by the Mayor, or when
called into Committee session by the Director, and shalt continue to carry out its functions,
powers and duties as provided by law.
This Order shall take effect immediately.
m2-6
ABRAHAM D. BEAME, Mayor.
