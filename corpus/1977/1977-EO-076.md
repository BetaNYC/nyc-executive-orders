EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 76
FEBRUARY 10, 1977
Establishment of a Westway Expediting Council
Whereas, Westway is the single most important capital project in the City; and
4 Whereas, This Administration has given top priority to the advancement of this project ;
an
Whereas, Westway received approval from the Secretary of Transportation, enabling
the City and State to proceed with planning and development.
Now, therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of The City of New York, it is
hereby ordered as follows:
Section 1,
A Westway
Expediting Council is hereby created, consisting of the First
Deputy Mayor, who shall
be Chairman, the Transportation Administrator, the Chairman
of the City Planning Commission, the Corporation Counsel and the Director of the Office
of Management and Budget.
§2.
The Transportation Administrator will coordinate all technical activities relating
to the expeditious preparation of the official roadway map and the Planning Commission
Chairman will coordinate land use and planning activities.
The Corporation Counsel and the Director of the Office of Management and Budget
will provide appropriate support services.
§
All agencies are directed to assign top policy level personnel to assist in this effort
when requested by the Council. In particular, the Commissioner of Sanitation, the Administrator of Environmental Protection, the Commissioner of Water Resources, the Administrator of Municipal Services and the Commissioner vf Ports and Terminals are requested
to attend Council meetings as required, and work directly with the members to carry out
the goals and objectives of building Westway under the overall direction of the First Deputy
Mayor or his designated representative.
§ 4.
This Order shall take effect immetliatety.
