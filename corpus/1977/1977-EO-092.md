ESTABLISHMENT OF AN OFFICE OF COMPUTER PLANS AND CONTROLS
IN THE OFFICE OF THE DIRECTOR OF OPERATIONS
Whereas, It is necessary to coordinate computer system development in The City of New
York in order to provide for the development of systems at least cost which will improve agency
operations, service levels and management information and these systems have common applicability to more than one agency or require data which is collected by more than one agency;
and it is necessary that the City develop and maintain a coordinated plan for the effective and
efficient use of information systems and insure that agency plans are consistent with this plan.
Now, therefore, pursuant to the authority invested in me as Mayor of The City of New
York, it is hereby ordered:

and Controls to be headed by the Director of Operations which will insure the elimination o
