EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 86
JUNE, 1977
AMENDMENT OF EXECUTICE ORDER NO. 28 DATED MARCH 31, 1975
Whereas, It is necessary and desirable to amend Executive Order No. 28, dated March
31, 1975, which established a Commission on the Status of Women.
Now, therefore, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:
Section 1,
Section 1} of the Executive Order No, 28 dated March 31, 1975, is hereby
amended to read as follows :
“Section |.
There is hereby established under the direction of the Office of the
Mayor a Commission on the Status of Women, which shall consist of thirty-eight
members appointed by the Mayor, including a Chairperson and a Vice-Chairperson
who shall serve at the pleasure of the Mayor, the Chairperson of the New York City
Commission on Human Rights who shall serve ex officio and thirty-five members who
shall serve as follows: twelve members shall be appointed for terms expiring on December 31, 1977; twelve shall be appointed
for terms expiring on December 31, 1978;
and eleven shall be appointed for terms expiring on December 31, 1979. Thereafter, all
members other than the Chairperson, Vice-Chairperson and ex officio member shall be
appointed for terms of three years. Should a vacaticy occur prior to the expiration
of the term of any member, such vacancy shall be filled for the unexpired term.”
§2,
This Order shall take effect immediately.

ABRAHAM D. BEAIME, Mayor.
