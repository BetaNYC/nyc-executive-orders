THE City OF NEW YORK
Orrice oF THE Mayor
New Yorn, NY 10007
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 20
March 23, 1995
RATOR
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered:
Section 1. Health Services Administrator, Maria Mitchell shall succeed to the
wers and duties of the Health Services Administrator as specified in Chapter 1016 of
the Laws of 1969 (Section 7381 et, seq. of the Unconsolidated Laws of New York), and
is hereby designated the official to act on behalf of the City of New York, in accordance
with subdivision 1 of section 8 of the Facilities Development Act (Chapter 359 of the
Laws of 1968), for the purpose of executing amendments to sublease agreements
heretofore made by and among the New York State Housing Finance Agency, the Health
and Mental Hygiene Facilities Improvement Corporation and the City of New York,
acting through the Health Services Administration.
Section 2, Prior
Order revoked, Executive Order No. 3, dated January 11,
1994, is hereby revoked.
Section 3. Effective Date. This Order shall take effect immediately.
Rudolph W. Giufiani
Mayor
