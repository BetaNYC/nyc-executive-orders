EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 52
November 10, 2004

TRANSFER OF FUNCTIONS WITH RESPECT TO THE
WNYE TELEVISION AND RADIO STATIONS AND ASSIGNMENT OF THE
LICENSES OF SUCH STATIONS

WHEREAS, section 1072(p) of the Charter grants the New York City Department of
Information Technology and Telecommunications (“DOITT”) the power to perform such
responsibilities with respect to information technology and telecommunications as the Mayor
shall direct;

WHEREAS, the WNYE television and radio stations are currently operated by the City
School District of the City of New York (doing business as the “Board of Education of the City
of New York” or the “New York City Department of Education” and hereafter referred to as
“DOE”);

WHEREAS, DOITT operates NYC TV, providing programming on cable television
educational and governmental channels;

WHEREAS, in an effort to enhance the quality of programming and to promote the
effective coordination of telecommunications resources, DOE and DOITT intend to complete the
assignment of the licenses for the WNYE radio and television stations from DOE to DOITT; and

WHEREAS, on October 26, 2004, the Federal Communications Commission granted its
consent to such assignment, subject to its completion in accordance with such consent;
NOW, THEREFORE, by the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is
hereby ordered:
Section 1. Effective December 12, 2004, or as soon as practicable thereafter, the function
of operating the WNYE television and radio stations and ancillary functions, including but not
limited to staffing, program development, day-to-day operations and overall supervision and
management, shall be transferred from DOE to DOITT.

§ 2. DOITT is hereby authorized to make arrangements for the assignment by DOE to
DOITT of the licenses for the WNYE television and radio stations on December 12, 2004, or as
soon as practicable thereafter, and to assume responsibilities with respect to such stations
consistent with such assignment.
§ 3. To the extent practicable, prior to the date of the transfer of functions, DOE and
DOITT may make appropriate arrangements, with the purpose of ensuring continuity in
operating the WNYE television and radio stations, with respect to: (a) use by DOITT of DOE
facilities and real and personal property in furtherance of this Order; (b) appropriate disposition
of contracts and agreements entered into by DOE for the benefit of such stations; and (c)
appropriate disposition of accounts receivable with respect to station operations.
§ 4. The Department of Citywide Administrative Services and DOITT shall take all steps
necessary, consistent with applicable law, to implement this Order, including ensuring the
transfer, pursuant to section 70(2) of the Civil Service Law, of employees substantially engaged
in the performance of the functions to be assumed pursuant to this Order. Employees who are
subject to pending disciplinary charges on the date of the functional transfer, or against whom a
disciplinary penalty has been assessed but not yet served or paid on or prior to such date, shall be
retained in the employment of DOE until the resolution of the adjudicative or administrative
proceedings and until any outstanding disciplinary penalty has been served or paid.
§ 5. In accordance with applicable law, employees of DOE, upon becoming employees
of the City, may elect to transfer their pension service credit from the Board of Education
Retirement System (“BERS”) to the New York City Employees Retirement System or may elect
to remain in BERS as transferred contributors.
§ 6. This Order shall be construed and implemented in a manner consistent with federal,
state and local laws and regulations, including but not limited to the Federal Communications
Act of 1934, as amended.
§ 7. The transfer of functions and the assignment of the WNYE radio and television
station licenses authorized and directed by this Order shall be contingent upon the written
concurrence of the Chancellor of DOE at least twenty days before the effective date of such
transfers.
§ 8. This Order shall take effect immediately.
