EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 49
September 13, 2004

AMENDMENT OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 47, DATED JANUARY 4, 1993,
RELATING TO CONTINUATION OF OFFICE OF FILM, THEATRE AND
BROADCASTING

By virtue of the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it
is hereby ordered:

Section 1.
Section one of Executive Order No. 47, dated January 4,
1993, is amended to read as follows:
Section 1.
Continuation of Office of Film, Theatre and Broadcasting.
There is continued within the Office of the Mayor an Office of Film, Theatre and
Broadcasting, to be headed by a commissioner who shall be appointed by and serve at the
pleasure of the Mayor. The Office of Film, Theatre and Broadcasting may also be known
as and identify itself as NYC Entertainment. The commissioner shall report to and be
directly accountable to the Deputy Mayor for Economic Development and Rebuilding.

§ 2. Effective Date. This order shall take effect immediately.

__________________________
Michael R. Bloomberg
Mayor
