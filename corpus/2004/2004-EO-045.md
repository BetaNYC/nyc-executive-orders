EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 45

FEBRUARY 24, 2004

A CHARGE FOR PAYMENT TO THE CITY BY CHECKS BY A BANK OR DEPOSITORY
INSTITUTION

By virtue of the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of New York, it is hereby
ordered:
Section 1.
Section 1 of Executive Order No. 125, dated December 4, 1989, is
amended to read as follows:
Section 1.
Charge of Payment on Account of Dishonored Check. Pursuant to section
85 of the General Municipal Law, a charge of twenty dollars per check may be added to any
account owing to the City of New York or any of its agencies where a tendered payment of such
account was by a check or other written order that was dishonored by a bank or depository
institution.
Section 2.
Effective Date. This Order shall take effect 30 days after publication in the
City Record.

MICHAEL R. BLOOMBERG
