THE City oF NEW YorK
OFFICE oF THE MAYOR
New York, N.Y. 10007
Executive Order No.
65
July 1, 1982
By the power vested in me as Mayor of the City of
New York, it is hereby ordered:
Section 1.
Designation.
The Office of the Coordinator of Criminal Justice is designated to act as the
planning and administrative agency for the City of New
York under the provisions of the Juvenile Justice and
Delinquency Prevention Act of 1974, as amended.
Section 2.
Effective Date.
This Order shall take
effect immediately.
